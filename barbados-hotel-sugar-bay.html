Sugar Bay

Idyllic surroundings, Bajan bustle and luxury facilities meet at Sugar Bay Barbados, a beachfront all-inclusive resort. Set on 150 meters of a white sand beach and bordering an ocean lagoon, Sugar Bay is a tranquil slice of paradise, yet the lively capital of Bridgetown, and some of the island’s most popular tourist attractions, is just 10 minutes away. On site, guests can enjoy four restaurants, five bars and two swimming pools.

Sugar Bay is located within a UNESCO World Heritage Site, the Historic Garrison. Barbados’ international airport can be reached in under 15 minutes. The popular tourist attractions of George Washington House, the South Coast Boardwalk and Garrison Savannah Horseracing track are all nearby.

Rooms at Sugar Bay

Sugar Bay encompasses 138 rooms and suites, each featuring stylish boho-inspired décor and modern conveniences. Bedrooms are furnished with comfy beds, cable TV, air conditioning, iPod docking stations and in-room tea and coffee facilities. The bathrooms come equipped with luxury amenities by The White Company. All accommodation has free Wi-Fi access and a private balcony or terrace.

Room types

  • Signature Room: Colourful and cosy, ideal for couples. Max two adults.
  • Deluxe Signature: Extra spacious with the addition of a living area complete with sleeper sofa and a larger bathroom. Max three adults.
    • Signature Rooms offer balcony views of the lush tropical gardens or the scenic pool area.
  • Oceanfront Room: The spectacular sea views are complemented by ocean-inspired décor. Max two adults.
  • Oceanfront Honeymoon Suite: Ideal for newlyweds, this suite is romantic and luxurious with a king-sized four-poster bed. Max two adults.
  • Deluxe Oceanfront Room: A roomy suite with a spacious living area complete with sleeper sofa and a large bathroom. Max three adults.
    • Oceanfront Rooms provide panoramic views of the Caribbean Sea.
  • Family Suite: Features a separate bedroom for privacy and a large living room complete with a sofa bed. The furnished balcony offers garden or pool views. Max four adults and one child.
  • Oceanfront Family Suite: Elegantly designed with a separate living room and spacious modern bathroom. The balcony provides sweeping views of the sea. Max four adults and one child.
  • Two Bedroom Suite: Ensures privacy for larger groups with two separate bedrooms and a large living/dining area. The balcony boasts scenic pool or garden views. Max five adults.
  • Two Bedroom Oceanfront Suite: The resort’s most spacious suite, large parties can relax in comfort with two separate bedrooms and a roomy living/dining area. The balcony provides panoramic ocean views.
    • Family Suites are spacious, ideal for those travelling in larger groups.

Bar Services at Sugar Bay

The all-inclusive package includes premium alcoholic and non-alcoholic beverages, best enjoyed in one of the resort’s five atmospheric bars. Guests can sip a mojito in the pool at the Swim-Up Pool Bar or enjoy a cool beer in a comfy armchair at The Lazy Mongoose Pub. Colin’s Beach Bar & Grill is modelled after a local rum shop and oozes Caribbean charm.

Dining at Sugar Bay

Sugar Bay is home to four different restaurants as well as an onsite café to satisfy all guests’ cravings.

  • Reef Restaurant: A relaxed beachside restaurant serving up a buffet breakfast, light lunches, afternoon tea and international buffet dinners.
  • olin’s Beach Bar & Grill: A casual grill specialising in Caribbean treats.
  • Sizzle Steakhouse: A popular steak restaurant with a focus on using quality local ingredients, superb cuts of beefs and Caribbean flavours. Sizzle Steakhouse is one of the resorts speciality restaurants and reservations are required.
  • UMI: An Asian-fusion restaurant with chic décor and excellent views of the resort. UMI is one of the resorts speciality restaurants and reservations are required.
  • The Coffee Bean: A lively café offering a delectable selection of bagels, croissants, soups, salads, cakes and more. (Not included in the all-inclusive package.)

AN ARRAY OF ACTIVITIES TO ENJOY

Along with comfy sundrenched loungers, Sugar Bay provides guests with an assortment of more active facilities. The all-inclusive resort is home to two swimming pools, a fitness centre and an arcade. Competitive types will enjoy the games room, where guests challenge each other to games of table football, air hockey, pool and video games. Access to non-motorised water sports equipment is also provided as part of the all-inclusive package. Younger visitors are well catered for with a crèche, kids club and teen clubhouse.

Excursions at Sugar Bay

Sugar Bay encourages its guests to get out and discover the beautiful island of Barbados. A number of excursions can be arranged through the resort activities’ desk. Alternatively, hire a car through the car rental desk and do it yourself. Sugar Bay sits within easy reach of Barbados’ best-loved attractions.

  • Atlantis Submarines Barbados: A submarine adventure through the Caribbean underworld.
  • The Barbados Wildlife Reserve: Home to some of the country’s most interesting creatures, including the Barbados Green Monkey.
  • Historic Bridgetown and its Garrison: An exceptional example of British colonial architecture, recognised as a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
  • Black Pearl Party Cruises: A pirate-themed party ship with rum punch, music and a rope swing.
  • Snorkelling: A 5-hour lunch cruise with the opportunity to swim amongst turtles and mysterious wrecks.

Sugar Bay Spa

The Karma Spa offers a wide range of treatments designed to relax and energise. The highly trained staff use top-of-the-line ELEMIS products which can be purchased in the hotel lobby. Choose from massages, facials, pedicures and more or simply relax in the sauna.

Weddings at Sugar Bay

The friendly Sugar Bay wedding team are on hand to help guests plan their dream big day. Brides and grooms to be can choose from a variety of wedding packages, including a complimentary service, and tailor the ceremony and reception to their exact needs. All the little details are taken care of, from the bouquet to breakfast in bed the next morning, for stress-free nuptials.
