Hard Rock Cancun

Designed to give guests the rock star treatment, Hard Rock Cancun is a luxurious playground of fun, sun and excess. Facilities, including four swimming pools and a spa, are totally modern with Hard Rock’s glitzy signature style. The 601 guest rooms are befitting of a celebrity, with deluxe bedding and custom-designed furniture sourced from far-flung corners of the world. The all-inclusive package includes meals at five international restaurants and drinks from five bars.
Watch the video and see for yourself. Hard Rock Cancun provides heaps of fun for the whole family, all day and all night. The facilities and service are out-of-this-world, to delight even the fussiest rock star diva.

Hard Rock Cancun sits on the beach in the heart of the city’s Hotel Zone. Restaurants, bars and shops are all within easy reach and the airport is less than 20-minutes away.

Rooms at Hard Rock Cancun

Stylish and sumptuous, Hard Rock guest rooms and suites have the star factor. Luxurious features include hydro-spa bathtubs, flat screen LCD televisions, Egyptian cotton sheets and liquor dispensers. Every room boasts its own balcony or terrace, many featuring picturesque ocean views. Modern conveniences such as a coffee maker, hair dryer and Wi-Fi are provided and bathroom amenities are top of the line.

  • Deluxe Gold: Available with one king or two double beds. Boasts a lagoon view. 430 square foot.
  • Deluxe Platinum: Available with one king or two double beds. Boasts an ocean view. 430 square foot.
  • Deluxe Diamond: Features one king bed. Enjoys an upgraded liquor selection, enhanced coffee machine and late-night pillow menu. Boasts an ocean view. 430 square foot.
  • Deluxe Family (Two Bedroom): Features two inter-connecting rooms, one with a king bed and another with two double beds. Comes equipped with an Xbox 360 with games on request, special kids amenities and beach toys. Boasts a lagoon view. 763 square foot.

Bar Services at Hard Rock Cancun

Hard Rock Cancun’s bars and lounges deliver a divine drinking experience, offering a world-class collection of vintage wine, hand-crafted cocktails and refreshing beers. Guests can sip their tipple of choice at one of two swim-up bars, one pool bar, a lobby lounge or theatre bar.

Dining at Hard Rock Cancun

Boasting six restaurants with dishes from around the globe, guests can always find an eatery to suit their mood. Room service is included and available around the clock.

  • Zen: Asian-themed fine dining, featuring Teppanyaki.
  • Ciao: A casual Italian trattoria with a buffet breakfast and a la carte dinners.
  • Ipanema: Brazilian steakhouse with fine cuts of beef, pork and poultry. Meat is freshly sliced at the table.
  • The Market: An international buffet with dishes from Mexico to Malaysia.
  • Pizzeto: Artisanal brick-oven pizza is served alongside other Mediterranean dishes.
  • Frida: An authentic Mexican restaurant with a traditional a la carte menu.

Mexican Hospitality

Mexico’s renowned beauty and warmth of spirit collide in this spectacular beachfront resort. Providing the opportunity for romance, relaxation and revelry, Hard Rock Cancun is the perfect destination to unwind.

Entertainment at Hard Rock Cancun

As you might expect from a hotel centred around hedonism, the entertainment last until wee hours at the Hard Rock Cancun. Foam parties are held weekly and expert DJs make regular visits. Look out for Rockstar Chef events, featuring show cooking paired with a killer soundtrack.

Activities at Hard Rock Cancun

In keeping with the rock star theme of this all-inclusive resort, Hard Rock Cancun offers a number of exciting musical experiences. Guests can live out their Hendrix fantasies and order electric guitars to their room from a choice of twenty different Fenders. In-room video tutorials are available or guests can take advantage of complimentary expert guidance in the Music Lab, where they can join a band and be coached towards an on-stage performance, music video or lay down a demo.

Teenage guests will enjoy the Cavern Club with table football and chill-out zones while kids are kept entertained and active with the Little Big Club. Guests also have access to a stretch of pristine beach, four swimming pools, sports facilities and a state-of-the-art gym.

Excursions at Hard Rock Cancun

Ancient treasures, lush jungle, pristine beaches and world-class golf courses are all within easy reach at the Hard Rock Cancun. The resort offers a number of tours to the Mayan Riviera’s top tourist attractions and as well as golf trips, lessons and club hire.

  • Chichen Itza
  • Tulum Express
  • Boogie Surf Dolphin Ride
  • Cancun Island Tour
  • Paradise Sub-See and Snorkel
  • Jungle Tour
  • Aquatwister
  • Combo Tulum and Coba
  • Boca del Puma Zip Line
  • Tankah
  • Royal Garrafon Rock Pass
  • Galleon Columbus Dinner

Hard Rock Cancun Spa

The resort’s Rock Spa provides offers a number of decadent treatments to revive the mind, body and soul. Massages, facials, scrubs and more can take place in a private treatment room, couples treatment room or outdoor palapa. An onsite beauty salon offers hair and beauty services.

Weddings at Hard Rock Cancun

Hard Rock Cancun offers nine different lavish wedding packages, designed by celebrity event designer Colin Cowie and coordinated by the resort’s expert wedding planners. Couples can tie the knot on the sand in front of an unlimited number of friends and family. Two scenic terraces, a ballroom and the beach are available for receptions.
