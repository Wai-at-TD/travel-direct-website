Hard Rock Cancun

Stretching along a quarter mile of white-sand beach, Gran Caribe provides all-inclusive luxury in one of Mexico's most scenic spots. Its varied assortment of five-star facilities are designed to cater to all ages and vacation styles. Couples can enjoy relaxing in a Jacuzzi or sip a cocktail on the sundeck while families will be delighted by the Oki Splash Waterpark and mini golf course. The beach resort encompasses five restaurants and eight bars, with most boasting views of the Caribbean Sea.

Gran Caribe sits in the heart of Cancun’s Hotel Zone on the Yucatan Peninsula. The International Airport is around 25-minutes away.

Rooms at Hard Rock Cancun

Gran Caribe offers a diverse assortment of suites, designed for singles, couples, families and groups, each with luxurious beds and separate seating areas. All accommodation enjoys top-of-the-line conveniences and electronics, including LCD televisions with satellite channels and DVD player, Wi-Fi access and air-conditioning. Bathrooms are spacious with marble fixtures, vanities and hair dryers. Guests can grab a cool drink from their fully-stocked mini bar to enjoy on their private or semi-private terrace or balcony.

Bar Services at Hard Rock Cancun

Gran Caribe is home to six bars and two lounges offering domestic and international drinks for every mood and occasion.

  • Lobby Bar: Boasting spectacular ocean views, nightly live music and a fabulous cocktail menu, this is the perfect spot for an aperitif.
  • Trade Winds Beach Bar: An easy-going seaside spot, guests can sip drinks on a hammock, deck swing, lounge chaise or double beach bed. Snacks are served along beers, cocktails, wines and soft drinks.
  • Show Bar: Open during performances, guests can enjoy their tipple of choice while taking in a show.
  • Albatros: Attached to the Albatros restaurant, this is a chic poolside bar where expert mixologists serve colourful concoctions.
  • Gaviotas Bar: Attached to the Gaviotas restaurant, rum drinks are a particularly popular choice from the extensive drinks menu.
  • Maria’s Bar: Attached to the Maria’s restaurant, this Mexican-themed bar specialises in creative Margaritas – blending fresh fruit with fine tequilas.
  • 24 Hour Lounge: Guests are encouraged to help themselves to snacks and drinks at this all-day and night lounge, complete with pool table.
  • 24 Hour VIP Lounge: Premium drinks and treats are available at this enhanced lounge, exclusive to VIP guests. The lounge features a butler service, reading nooks and computer station.

Dining at Hard Rock Cancun

Buffet and a la carte, fine dining and quick eats, the all-inclusive package includes five diverse restaurants serving dishes from around the world. Guests can also enjoy meals in the privacy of their suite with room service available from 7:00 am to 11:00 pm.

  • Albatros: Dishes from around the globe are served both buffet style and a la carte. Regular features include seafood grilled over open flames, local delicacies and fresh meats.
  • Gaviotas: A beachfront restaurant where guests can watch the chefs in action with exhibition kitchens. Buffet and a la carte dishes are available, featuring Mexican and Caribbean twists.
  • Il Pescatore: An Italian-style restaurant specialising in seafood.
  • Maria’s: Authentic Mexican dishes are served in this hacienda-style eatery.
  • Sunset Grill: A favourite with families, this casual grill hosts kids shows three times a week.

Entertainment at Hard Rock Cancun

After admiring one of the Yucatan Peninsula’s famed sunsets, guests can enjoy the evening’s entertainment. The onsite theatre hosts live interactive shows every night, performed by the in-house activities team. After, guests are encouraged to don their dancing shoes and head to the Nitro Disco Club, where drinks are on the house until 2:00 am.

Activities at Hard Rock Cancun

Providing an enriching and entertaining holiday experience, Hard Rock Cancun plays host to a variety of activities to suit all ages. The all-inclusive resort encompasses a range of sports facilities -- including two floodlit tennis courts, a multi-purpose court and beach volleyball court -- and a large pool where water polo and aqua aerobics take place. Guests can join a scuba diving demonstration, compete in a ping pong tournament, learn how to boogie board and much more.

The Kids Paradise Programme offers daily activities for children aged 4 to 12 as well as night time fun, such as glow parties, karaoke nights and Mexican fiestas. The Jetix Club ensures teens are taken care of too, with friendly matches, video games, films and non-motorised water sports. The resorts tiniest visitors will enjoy Baby Paradise, a complimentary nursery with an indoor and outdoor play area.

Excursions at Hard Rock Cancun

Rich in history, scenery and adventure the Yucatan Peninsula is a traveller's delight. Hard Rock Cancun can arrange a number of tours throughout the region, covering some of Mexico's top tourist attractions.

  • Snorkelling and Scuba Diving
  • Xcaret
  • Tulum
  • Chichen Itza
  • Swimming with Dolphins

Hard Rock Cancun Spa

The resort’s luxury spa offers a range of treatments and facilities for guests seeking the ultimate in relaxation. Facials, massages, scrubs, hydrotherapies and salon services are all on offer in a truly sumptuous setting.

Weddings at Hard Rock Cancun

Couples dreaming of a wedding in paradise will be delighted by this luxury resort’s range of comprehensive wedding packages. The bride and groom can declare their everlasting love for one another with their toes in the sand of a picturesque beach before enjoying their romantic honeymoon at Hard Rock Cancun .
