Hyatt Zilara Rose Hall

An adults-only oasis of calm, Hyatt Zilara Rose Hall is the perfect destination for a relaxing escape. The all-inclusive resort is set on the same grounds as Hyatt Ziva Rose Hall but, unlike the Ziva property, is exclusively for guests aged 18 and above. With glorious views of the ocean and Jamaican Blue Mountains, the resort’s extensive grounds include 234 suites, three restaurants and three bars. Guests also enjoy access to all bars and restaurants at Hyatt Ziva Rose Hall.
Watch the video and see for yourself. Hyatt Zilara Rose Hall delivers the ultimate all-inclusive package through gourmet restaurants, trendy bars and world-class facilities. The resort’s attentive and friendly staff provide “service from the heart”.

Hyatt Zilara Rose Hall sits on the picturesque shores of Montego Bay, on the north coast of Jamaica. The resort is just 15 minutes away from Sangster International Airport and five-minutes from some of the island’s finest golf courses.

Rooms at Hyatt Zilara Rose Hall

Hyatt Zilara is home to 234 elegant suites, each equipped with luxury beds, modern bathrooms and fully-furnished balconies or patios. State-of-the-art facilities are provided, including air conditioning, LCD satellite televisions and iPod docking stations. A fully-stocked mini bar and 24-hour room service are provided part of the all-inclusive package.

  • Standard: Available with a king or two double beds. Terrace offers views of the pool, gardens or partial views of the Caribbean Sea. 493 square feet.
  • Ocean View: Available with a king or two double beds. Features views of the Caribbean Sea. 493 square feet.
  • Ocean Front: Features two double beds. Boasts sweeping views of the Caribbean Sea. 493 square feet.
  • Swim-Up: Available with a king or double bed. Provides direct access to the swimming pool. 492 square feet.
  • Junior Suite: Available with a king or two double beds. 673 square feet.
  • Ocean View Junior Suite: Features a king-sized bed. The private balcony overlooks the Caribbean Sea. 673 square feet.
  • Ocean Front Junior Suite: Features a king-sized bed. Boasts sweeping views of the Caribbean Sea. 673 square feet.
  • Swim Up Junior Suite: Available with a king or two double beds. Provides direct access to the swimming pool. 673 square feet.
  • One Bedroom Ocean View Butler Suite: Features a separate bedroom with a king-sized bed. Guests enjoy a personalised butler service. Terrace overlooks the Caribbean Sea. 1,012 square feet.
  • One Bedroom Ocean Front Butler Suite: Features a separate bedroom with an ocean-facing king-sized bed. Guests enjoy a personalised butler service. Boasts sweeping views of the Caribbean Sea. 1,145 square feet.
  • Ocean Front Grand Butler Suite: Features a separate bedroom with an ocean-facing king-sized bed. Guests enjoy a personalised butler service. Boasts sweeping views of the Caribbean Sea. 1,201 square feet.

Bar Services at Hyatt Zilara Rose Hall

Hyatt Zilara is home to a selection of sophisticated adults-only bars. Guests also enjoy access to four additional Hyatt Ziva bars.

  • PotionZ: Inspired by local ghost story, the White Witch of Rose Hall, PotionZ has a magical ambiance and playful cocktail menu.
  • IslandZ: Situated within the pool, guests can swim-up and order a cool one.
  • Fez: This intimate rooftop bar has a colourful Moroccan theme.
Within Hyatt Ziva:
  • Shakerz: An open-air bar specialising in cocktails.
  • Grindz: Fine Jamaican coffee is served with pastries and snacks.
  • Lagoonz: Draft beer, wine and signature cocktails can all be enjoyed without ever leaving the pool.
  • Stirz: Set in the luxurious lobby, this is the perfect place to sip an expertly mixed cocktail.

Dining at Hyatt Zilara Rose Hall

A gourmet dining experience is an essential part of the Hyatt Zilara package. Chefs combine fresh local ingredients with culinary traditions that span the globe. As well as several fine restaurants, guests will find food carts dotted throughout the resort.

  • Petit PariZ: Sophisticated French fare is the specialty of this upscale restaurant.
  • FlavorZ: A gourmet buffet, with a particularly fine selection of fresh fruit and vegetables.
  • HoriZonZ: Steak and seafood are served in a waterfront setting.
Within Hyatt Ziva:
  • Barefoot Jerkz: A traditional jerk shack located on the beach.
  • Bitez: Deli-style sandwiches are made with fine meats, cheeses and vegetables.
  • Brazil: A Brazilian-style steak house; the beef skewers are renowned.
  • Calypzo: An open-air restaurant with a menu of Caribbean treats and a popular daiquiri bar.
  • Choicez: This diverse international restaurant boasts three show cooking stations.
  • Di Roza: Clay-oven pizzas and fine pasta dishes are the staples of this Italian trattoria.
  • Fuzion: A delicious taste of the East, the Fuzion menu features a diverse assortment of Asian cuisine.
  • Union Jack’z: A sports pub serving traditional British favourites.

Entertainment at Hyatt Zilara Rose Hall

Guests can round off a perfect day in the sun with an evening dancing under the stars. Hyatt Zilara provides a wealth of entertainment options, including stellar live music performances. The Street Generation Jamaica traces the history of the country’s music while the International Show features rhythms from all over the world. The Z Beach Party is an opportunity to put your limbo skills to the test and enjoy authentic Caribbean beats. Ask about the performances taking place during your stay upon check in.

Activities at Hyatt Zilara Rose Hall

There’s plenty of ways to fill sun-drenched days at Hyatt Zilara, with water sports, fitness classes, sports matches and much more all on offer. Alternatively, relax by the pool or on the beach.

The resort sits adjacent to both the White Witch Golf Course and Cinnamon Hill Golf Course, where guests can enjoy a complimentary round in stunning surroundings.

Excursions at Hyatt Zilara Rose Hall

Those who want to venture further afield can arrange a variety of adventure and culture trips through the resort’s chosen tour operator, Island Routes. Available excursions include a reggae catamaran cruise, dolphin encounter and swamp village tour.

Hyatt Zilara Rose Hall Spa

Situated by the ocean, the onsite Zen Spa is haven of relaxation and wellness. Highly-trained staff offer an array of treatments, including massages, body wraps, facial and aromatherapy in an indoor or outdoor setting. The spa’s steam room, sauna and plunge pools can be accessed as part of the all-inclusive package. Spa and salon treatments are available at an extra cost.

Weddings at Hyatt Zilara Rose Hall

Hyatt Zilara employs a team of wedding specialists, dedicated to helping guests achieve the Caribbean wedding of their dreams. They offer a range of packages to suit different tastes and budgets, with all the details taken care of. Say “I do” in an intimate beach ceremony or an elaborate ballroom party. Vow renewal and anniversary packages are also available.
