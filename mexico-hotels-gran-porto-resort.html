Gran Porto Resort

Rising from a wide stretch of protected beach, Gran Porto resort welcomes couples, families, friends and singles to enjoy a luxury all-inclusive experience. Featuring traditional Mexican architecture, the hacienda-style property encompasses 287 guest rooms, five restaurants, three bars, two swimming pools and a deluxe spa. Its superb Playa del Carmen location grants guests easy access to a host of amenities.

Gran Porto Hotel is set in the Mayan Riviera, on the sandy Playa del Carmen beach. The chic restaurants, clubs and shops of bustling Fifth Avenue are less than a five-minute walk away. The International Airport can be reached in half an hour.

Rooms at Gran Porto Resort

Gran Porto offers a wealth of well-appointed accommodation, designed to ensure the comfort of singles, couples, families and groups. Each suite features a terrace or balcony and luxurious full-size bathroom. Modern conveniences are supplied, including a large LCD television with DVD player, coffeemaker, hairdryer and Wi-Fi. The in-suite mini bar is stocked daily.

  • Standard: Features two double beds and a semi-private terrace. 263 square foot.
  • Junior Suite: Available with one king-sized or two double beds. Features a private terrace or balcony. 425 square foot.
  • Junior Suite Ocean View: Available with one king-sized or two double beds. Features a private terrace or balcony overlooking the ocean. Upgraded amenities are provided, including a domestic liquor cabinet and Nespresso machine. 520 square foot.
  • Junior Suite Ocean Front: Available with one king-sized or two double beds. Features a private terrace or balcony, complete with hammock, boasting uninterrupted views of the ocean. Upgraded amenities are provided, including a domestic liquor cabinet and Nespresso machine. 520 square foot.
  • Family Junior Suite Ocean Front: Features a king-sized bed, bunk beds a trundle. The private terrace or balcony, complete with hammock, boast uninterrupted views of the ocean. Upgraded amenities are provided, including a domestic liquor cabinet and Nespresso machine. 520 square foot.

These recently-refurbished Gran suites feature enhanced décor, royal bedding, a premium liquor cabinet and Bose stereo and speaker system with iPod. Guests staying in Gran suites enjoy a number of additional luxuries and privileges, including direct access to the beach, a designated beach cabana, nightly turndown service and all-inclusive services at the neighbouring Royal Playa del Carmen resort (excluding Chef’s Plate).

  • Gran Junior Suite Walk Out: Available with two king-sized or two double beds. The private terrace boasts superb views of the beach and Caribbean Sea. 562 square foot.
  • Gran Master One Bedroom Ocean Front: Features a king-sized bed in a separate oceanfront bedroom and a spacious living room. The private terrace boasts superb views of the beach and Caribbean Sea. The spa-style bathroom is supplied with indulgent Bvlgari products.

Bar Services at Gran Porto Resort

With a firm focus on bars and dining, Gran Porto offers a number of atmospheric venues to kick back with a cool one or enjoy a tete a tete over a cappuccino.

  • Tradewinds Beach Bar: A laidback spot where expertly-prepared cocktails are accompanied by snacks, fingers food, sandwiches and kebabs. Guests can relax on hammocks, deck swings, lounge chaises and double beach beds.
  • Gaviotas: An oceanfront bar with a diverse drinks menu. The rum cocktails perfectly complement the Caribbean treats on offer.
  • Lobby Bar: Boasting spectacular views of the Caribbean Sea, this lively lounge is arguably the hub of the resort. Live music plays every night.
  • 24-Hour All-Inclusive Club: Guests can enjoy snacks and drinks at any time of the day or night from this self-service lounge. Competitive types will enjoy the array of table games available.

Dining at Gran Porto Resort

Gran Porto’s five eateries serve fine food in a relaxed and welcoming setting. With dishes spanning continents, there’s something to suit every appetite. Guests staying in Gran suites have even more choice with access to six additional restaurants at The Royal Playa del Carmen. Room service is available from 7:00 am to 11:00 pm.

  • Albatros: Dishes from around the world are served a la carte and buffet style. The diverse array of dishes include seafood prepared to local recipes and cooked over an open flame.
  • Maria’s: An authentic Mexican restaurant with a warm and vibrant atmosphere.
  • Il Pescatore: This Italian trattoria specialises in fresh seafood dishes. The elegant bar area is the perfect spot for an aperitif.
  • Parillada Grill: A beachside barbeque open for lunch. Steaks, chicken, seafood and vegetables are cooked to perfection over a wood-fired grill.
  • Health & Sushi Bar: Focusing on food to make you feel good, this rooftop spot serves healthy bites buffet-style.

Mexican Hospitality

Entertainment at Gran Porto Resort

When the sun goes down, the party gets going at Gran Porto. The resort plays host to live dance and theatre shows every night and guests can enjoy musical performances Monday to Sunday in the Lobby Bar. Themed evenings are held regularly, including Mexican fiestas.

Activities at Gran Porto Resort

Guests of all-ages will find plenty to do at this all-inclusive resort, with a variety of sport and social activities held every day. The freshwater activity pool plays host to aqua aerobics sessions and the onsite gym features state-of-art weights and cardio equipment. Food and drink aficionados will be delighted by the wine and tequila tastings, cooking classes and demonstrations and bartending lessons on offer.

The complimentary kids and teens clubs help keep youngsters entertained and engaged. The Oki Kids Club is designed for children aged 4-12 years, with trained staff facilitating water sports sessions, sand-castle building, face painting, hula-hula classes, crafts and much more. T-Zone, aimed at 13-17 year olds, provides a space for teenage guests to socialise and have fun. The specially designed area features video games, pool tables and educational facilities. Mini Golf Fun World is popular with the whole family.

Excursions at Gran Porto Resort

Staff run daily bike tours of the bustling Fifth Avenue shopping district and a number of other downtown hotspots. Those looking to explore further afield can choose from a variety of trips and tours at the hotel excursions desk. Xcaret Ecological Theme Park is just 20 minutes-away and the ancient Mayan wonders of Chichen Itza and Tulum are easy day trips. Golf, dolphin swims, scuba diving, sailing and much more can also be arranged.

Gran Porto Resort Spa

The resort spa takes the luxury holiday experience to the next level, offering a range of indulgent treatments to leave guests in a state of pure bliss. The whirlpool tub, sauna, steam bath and cold plunge pool can all be accessed for free while massages, scrubs, facials, salon services and more can be enjoyed for an additional fee.

Weddings at Gran Porto Resort

The perfect backdrop for heartfelt vows, Gran Porto offers a range of romantic wedding packages and a la carte services. Couples can say “I do” in a stunning oceanfront gazebo or with their toes in the sand with a choice of two beaches. The ballroom, beach and two stunning restaurants are all available for receptions.
