Holiday Inn

Breathtaking scenery, superb amenities and an exciting array of activities combine at Holiday Inn Montego Bay. This beachside resort is a favourite amongst families thanks to its excellent kids’ facilities and friendly service. Your all-inclusive package allows access to nine onsite bars and restaurants, serving up food and drinks from around the world.

The resort sits on a half-mile stretch of white sandy beach and encompasses a private island. Lively downtown Montego Bay is just a 15-minute drive away.

Rooms at Holiday Inn

The property includes 518 rooms, each equipped with a spacious patio or balcony. Modern amenities, such in-room Wi-Fi, a flat-screen TV, hairdryer and coffee machine help to create a home away from home.

  • Standard Room: Features views of the manicured gardens or surrounding hills.
  • Superior Room: Offers partial views of the ocean.
  • Deluxe Room: Provides sweeping views of the Caribbean Sea.
  • One Bedroom Suite: Includes a separate living area, whirlpool tub and ocean views.
  • Junior Suite: Top-floor accommodation with enhanced furnishings, upgraded amenities and a partially-separated living area. The balconies overlook the gardens or ocean.

Bar Services at Holiday Inn

The all-inclusive resort encompasses three bars, each boasting a menu colourful Caribbean cocktails, soft drinks, beers and wine. Choose from a lively pub, adults-only poolside retreat or mellow lounge.

Dining Services At Holiday Inn

Delicious dining options abound at Holiday Inn, home to six different restaurants.

  • Portside Buffet Restaurant: This varied buffet includes both international and local dishes.
  • Sea Breeze: Set in a beach gazebo, guests can feast on grilled specialities while enjoying the view.
  • Sorrento: An indoor venue overlooking the ocean, this Italian restaurant provides guests with a fine dining experience.
  • De Terrace: Guests can enjoy a true taste of the Caribbean at this Jamaican restaurant.
  • Barefoot Bar & Grill: Serving up hamburgers and hotdogs, this is the perfect spot for a snack or informal lunch.
  • Kenko Japanese Restaurant: An a la carte restaurant where teppanyaki dishes are served with tricks and twists. This outlet is not part of the all-inclusive meal plan.

Jamaican Hospitality

Jamaica’s renowned beauty, cuisine and warmth of spirit collide in this spectacular beachfront resort. Providing the opportunity for romance, relaxation and revelry, Holiday Inn is the perfect destination to unwind.

Entertainment at Holiday Inn

Guests are treated to a different theme night, seven days a week. An exciting schedule of live entertainment, music and dancing keeps the tempo up at Holiday Inn.

Activities at Holiday Inn

The expansive resort grounds encompass four tennis courts, a nine-hole mini golf course and two swimming pools, providing plenty of opportunities to get active on holiday. Your all-inclusive package also includes access to non-motorised water sports equipment, such as snorkelling kits, kayaks and sunfish sail boats. Fitness fanatics will be delighted by the onsite gym, equipped with exercise bikes, stair steppers, treadmills, weights and a television.

Those with little ones can take advantage of Holiday Inn’s complimentary KidSpree Vacation Club, providing hours of cost-free games, activities and excursions for the resort’s youngest guests. The onsite game room is popular amongst older children.

Holiday Inn Spa

Set in the greenery of the garden courtyard, Sol-Mer Spa & Salon offers a number of relaxing and restoring treatments. Massages, body wraps and facials help guests to unwind while hairdressing services, manicures and pedicures are ideal for those who want to look and feel extra special on holiday.

Weddings at Holiday Inn

Couples looking to get hitched can look forward to a hassle-free “I do” thanks to the onsite wedding centre. Expert wedding coordinators can help you choose from a number of wedding packages and services, including a complimentary package. Ranging from intimate celebrations to big Jamaican parties, weddings at the Holiday Inn offer the romance and fun you can expect from the Caribbean.
