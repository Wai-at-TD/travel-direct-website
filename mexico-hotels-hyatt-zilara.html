Hyatt Zilara

Adults-only and all-inclusive, Hyatt Zilara Cancun is a place to leave your worries behind and embrace the calm of the Caribbean. Situated on a wide stretch of sandy beach, the architecture of the resort has been inspired by centuries of Mexican tradition yet facilities are undeniably modern. Accommodation is all-suite and guests enjoy access to six gourmet restaurants, five bars, two pools, and a state-of-the-art gym.

The Hyatt Zilara sits in the heart Cancun’s Zona Hotelera (Hotel Zone), just 15 minutes away from vibrant downtown Cancun and around half an hour from the international airport.

Rooms at Hyatt Zilara

The Hyatt Zilara Cancun is home to 307 suites, each featuring elegant décor, handcrafted furniture, spa-style bathrooms and private balconies or patios overlooking the Caribbean Sea. Top-of-the-line electronics are provided such as wide-screen LED televisions, DVD players and MP3 players. The all-inclusive package includes a complimentary in-suite refreshment centre and liquor cabinet.

  • Junior Suite: Available with a king-sized bed or two double beds. 653 square foot.
  • Ocean View Junior Suite: Available with a king-sized bed or two double beds. Enjoys enhanced ocean views. 653 square foot.
  • Ocean Front Junior Suite: Available with a king-sized bed or two double beds. Enjoys unobstructed ocean views. 653 square foot.

Guess staying in these Premium Suites are granted a number of special services and privileges including complimentary ironing, luggage unpacking, personalised butler and concierge services, a reserved beach cabana and access to Chef’s Plate gourmet restaurant:

  • Ocean View Swim Up Suite: Features a king-sized bed. Boasts direct access to the swimming pool, enhanced furnishings and a telescope for stargazing. 848 square foot split across two levels.
  • Ocean Front Luxury Suite: Features a king-sized bed. Boasts uninterrupted views of the ocean and enhanced furnishings. 653 square foot.
  • Presidential Suite: Features a king-sized bed. A penthouse suite with a separate living room and dining room, expanded terrace, fully-stocked bar and telescope for stargazing. 1,331 square feet.

Bar Services at Hyatt Zilara

This adults-only resort is home to five distinct bars, all serving up high-quality beverages in a relaxed setting. The all-inclusive package includes unlimited alcoholic and non-alcoholic beverages.

  • Pelicanos Beach Bar: Refreshing drinks are served overlooking the sparkling waters of the Caribbean.
  • Trade Winds Swim-Up Bar: The perfect spot for a cool drink, guests can sip beers, cocktails and more without leaving the pool.
  • Tequileria Beach Bar: Set on the sand, this casual bar features extensive list of Mexican tequilas and relaxing swing seats.
  • 24 Hour Lounge: A classic cocktail and snack lounge featuring popular table games such as billiards and ping pong. Open all night long.
  • Lobby Bar: Home to a martini corner, wine corner and tequileria, this is a varied bar with nightly music and superb views.

Dining at Hyatt Zilara

When hunger strikes, guests can choose to chow down at one of six fine restaurants, grab a snack at the cafe or order a meal to enjoy in the privacy of their suite. Each eatery has its own distinct menu and atmosphere, satisfying all palates and persuasions.

  • Asiana: Contemporary Asian cuisine featuring a Teppanyaki cooking show, sushi and sashimi.
  • Pelicanos: Gourmet Caribbean seafood in an oceanfront setting.
  • Maria Marie: Fine Mexican cuisine with an upscale atmosphere.
  • Tapas & Pintxos: A flavoursome assortment of Basque-style tapas, served in a cosy environment.
  • Spice: An international buffet of the highest quality. Options change at every meal though their famed guacamole remains a constant feature.
  • Chef’s Plate: The Executive Chef serves a seven-course set menu which changes every day of the week. This restaurant is only available to guests staying in Junior Ocean Front Suites or Premium Suites.
  • Coffee House: Espresso drinks are accompanied by a wide variety of pastries, desserts and ice cream.

Mexican Hospitality

Mexico’s renowned beauty and warmth of spirit collide in this spectacular beachfront resort. Providing the opportunity for romance, relaxation and revelry, Hyatt Zilara Cancun is the perfect destination to unwind.

Entertainment at Hyatt Zilara

When the sun sets, Hyatt Zilara lights up with the evening’s entertainment. With something scheduled for every night, guests can enjoy live music, dance performances, Broadway-style shows, wine tastings and much more. The pools regularly host parties with live DJs and the hotel lobby transforms into an immersive stage.

Activities at Hyatt Zilara

The all-inclusive resort offers guests a plethora of recreational activities and fitness and wellness services to enjoy on holiday. Start the day with a yoga class, join a game of beach volleyball, learn to make authentic Mexican guacamole or take a leisurely cycle ride. The resort encompasses a beachfront infinity pool and Olympic-size pool where water volleyball and aerobics take place.

Hyatt Zilara Spa

Overlooking the sparkling waters of the Caribbean Sea, the Hyatt Zilara is the only Cancun spa resort with ocean views. The Mayan-inspired retreat offers a number of eco-holistic treatments, including healing massages, invigorating body scrubs and cleansing facials. Guests can also choose from a range of salon services, such as manicures, pedicures, cut and styles and blowouts. Access to the hydrotherapy circuit (steam room, sauna and cold plunge pool), whirlpool tub and fitness centre is complimentary.

Weddings at Hyatt Zilara

Offering beaches, ballrooms, gazebos and gardens, Hyatt Zilara Cancun is the ultimate romantic destination for your dream day. The resort is fully equipped with an onsite magistrate office and justice of the peace, along with a range of ceremony and reception venues. Dedicated wedding planners help couples to choose from comprehensive packages and tailor the event to their exact specifications, helping with everything from buttons holes to bubbly.
