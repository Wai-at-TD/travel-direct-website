Excellence Riviera Cancun

A taste of the sublime on Mexico’s Caribbean coast, Excellence Riviera is an all-adults, all-suite and all-inclusive resort. Six pools are nestled amongst the lush greenery of the tropical gardens and a number of hotel facilities sit on the resort’s picturesque stretch of beach. The 442 suites take luxury to a whole new level with sumptuous furnishings and top-of-the-line facilities. As part of the all-inclusive package, guests have access to nine restaurants, ten bars, a state-of-the-art fitness centre and wide range of activities.

An oasis of tranquillity in vibrant Cancun, Excellence Riviera is secluded without being isolated. The luxury resort sits on a white sand beach amongst the gorgeous Caribbean scenery of the Mayan Riviera. Cancun airport is just 15 minutes away.

Rooms at Excellence Riviera Cancun

Each of Excellence Riviera’s beautifully-furnished suites come equipped with high-quality amenities, air-conditioning, a fully-stocked mini bar and a King or two double beds. The full marble bathrooms feature jetted-whirlpool tubs, separate showers, separate water closets and double vanities. Complimentary Wi-Fi is available in every room.

  • Junior Suite: Overlooks the spa or pool. 668 square feet.
  • Junior Swim-up Suite: Features an additional exterior Jacuzzi, lounge bed and hammock. Located on the ground floor with direct access to the pool. 668 square feet.
  • Junior Suite with Ocean View: Overlooks the ocean. 668 square feet.

Excellence Club Suites

Guests staying in one of the Excellence Club Suites enjoy a wide range of additional services and benefits, including private check-in and check-out, private concierge service, a private lounge, superior toiletries, a private beach area and an upgraded mini bar.

  • Excellence Club Junior Suite: Ocean front suite. Features an additional exterior Jacuzzi, lounge bed and hammock. Located on the ground floor with direct access to pool. 668 square feet.
  • Excellence Club Junior Swim-up Suite: Overlooks the ocean. Features an additional exterior Jacuzzi.
  • Excellence Club Two-Story Rooftop Terrace Suite: Ocean front suite. Features an additional exterior Jacuzzi, plunge pool and shower on a private roof deck (heated in the winter). 1,058 square feet.
  • Honeymoon Suite: Ocean front suite. Features two balconies and an additional exterior Jacuzzi. 1,041 square feet.
  • Honeymoon Suite with Rooftop Terrace: Ocean front suite. Features two balconies, one with an additional exterior Jacuzzi. Includes a private roof deck (heated in winter) with plunge pool and open-air shower. 1,041 square feet.
  • One-Bedroom Imperial Suite: Ocean front suite. Features a separate dining room, separate living room, separate kitchen and an additional exterior Jacuzzi. 1,192 square feet.

Bar Services at Excellence Riviera Cancun

The adults-only resort encompasses 11 distinct bars, catering to all tastes and moods. Enjoy a beer while watching the big match, a coffee with a good book, or cocktail while marvelling at yet another gorgeous Caribbean sunset. The all-inclusive package includes unlimited natural fruit juices, soft drinks and alcoholic beverages.

  • Martini: Located in the main lobby, Martini is a popular meeting spot. Patrons can enjoy live music while sipping an expertly-prepared cocktail.
  • Cafékafé: An outdoor bistro with an extensive menu that will delight all coffee lovers.
  • Blue: A swim-up bar, guests can pause for a cool drink without leaving the water.
  • Sol: This popular pool bar is an ideal spot for sunbathing and slurping.
  • Cielo: Exclusively for Excellence Club members, bed-style seating makes this a luxurious place to relax.
  • Aroma: This coffee house serves up tasty hot drinks along with a wide variety of pastries, muffins, cakes and ice cream.
  • Stars: Guests can enjoy a drink and show at this theatre bar.
  • Sports Bar: Delicious snacks, cool beer and a big screen make this a popular spot to watch all the important games.
  • Xlounge: Situated directly on the beach, this chilled-out bar is the perfect place to take in the view.
  • Revive: Colourful smoothies are prepared with fresh seasonal fruit.
  • La Habana: A cosy setting for premium cigars and spirits.

Dining at Excellence Riviera Cancun

Excellence Riviera features ten varied restaurants, taking guests on a gourmet trip around the world.

  • Agave: A lively restaurant serving up authentic Mexican cuisine and Tex-Mex favourites. Guests can enjoy their meal in the romantic interior or on the outdoor hacienda-style courtyard. Tableside mariachi entertainment completes the experience. Open 5:30 pm -10:30 pm.
  • Oregano: A trattoria-style restaurant doing what Italy does best, simple yet delicious dishes. Pasta and pizza are popular choices. Open 6:00 pm-11:00 pm.
  • Basmati: An elegant Indian restaurant specialising in food from the north-western region. Open 5:30 pm-10:30 pm.
  • Chez Isabelle:Offering sophisticated French cuisine, this fine-dining establishment has both indoor and outdoor seating with live music every night. Open 5:30 pm-10:30 pm.
  • The Grill: At lunch time, this casual eatery serves up salads, sandwiches, burgers and wraps. In the evening, guests can enjoy an alfresco dinner of steak, chicken or shrimp, cooked to perfection on an open flame. Lunch 12:00 pm-6:00 pm, dinner 6:30-11:00 pm.
  • Flavour Market: A trendy restaurant with a relaxed vibe and mouth-watering tapas dishes. Open 6:00 pm-12:00 am.
  • The Lobster House: Set in an open-air thatch-roof palapa, this relaxed restaurant offers shellfish dishes prepared precisely to guests’ tastes. Breakfast 8:00 am-12:00 pm, lunch 12:30 pm-3:00 pm and dinner 5:30 pm-10:30 pm.
  • Las Olas: Located on the beach, this popular grill offers fast food, salad, wood-oven pizzas and more. Open 12:00 pm– 3:00pm.
  • Spice:Guests can transport their taste buds to the far east with dishes from Japan, China, Vietnam and Thailand. The restaurant encompasses a sushi bar and Teppanyaki rooms. Open 5:30 pm-10:30 pm.
  • Toscana: This Italian restaurant features traditional dishes with contemporary twists. Breakfast and lunch are served buffet style while dinner is a la carte. Breakfast 7:00 am-11:00 am, lunch 12:00 pm-4:00 pm and dinner 5:30 pm-10:30 pm

Entertainment at Excellence Riviera Cancun

Live music is a regular occurrence across Excellence Riviera’s lively restaurants and atmospheric bars. Professional shows take place every night at the Star Bar, ranging from tribute acts to Mexican folklore enactments. The Sports Bar screens all major sporting events. Look out for special theme nights.

Activities at Excellence Riviera Cancun

The resort pulls out all the stops when it comes to keeping guests active and entertained. Sporty types can enjoy yoga, pilates, aerobics and aqua fitness classes as well as access to tennis, football and beach volleyball facilities. Introductory scuba, Spanish, dancing and cooking lessons are a great way to pick up a new skill on holiday.

Excursions at Excellence Riviera Cancun

Complimentary bicycle tours and a number of other extra-cost excursions provide an easy way to get out and explore Mexico. Excellence Riviera offers shared or private deep-sea fishing charters, golf trips, shipwreck dives, tastings and more.

Excellence Riviera Cancun Spa

Excellence Riviera is home to a world class spa and beauty parlour with a varied menu of relaxing and restorative treatments. Highly-trained professionals carry out facials, massages, scrubs, manicures, pedicures and rituals. The temazcal purification treatment uses a steaming method with pre-Hispanic origins.

Weddings at Excellence Riviera Cancun

Set amongst the splendour or Mexico’s Caribbean coast, there are few more romantic places to say “I do” than Excellence Riviera. Couples can exchange vows in a seafront gazebo, an ocean view terrace or with their toes buried in the soft white sand. The hotel’s comprehensive wedding packages take care of everything, from the minister to the groom’s buttonhole, for a stress-free big day.
