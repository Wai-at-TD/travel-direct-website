Situated on the beautiful seven-mile beach in Negril, 60 miles west of Montego Bay airport and 90 minutes by road or 15 minutes by air (aircraft transfers are not included). Couples Swept Away offers a secluded paradise for couples to relax and enjoy, whether you’re taking in an unhurried meal in an al fresco café, exploring the seven miles of pristine sandy beach, enjoying one of our fantastic island excursions or just having a well-deserved lounge by the pool, Couples Swept Away will steal your heart and free your mind.

Rooms at Couples Swept Away

Awash in romance and privacy are the 312 Caribbean-style rooms & suites, spread throughout 27 acres of tropical landscaping. All rooms feature a spacious private verandah or balcony with king size bed, air-conditioning, ceiling fan, telephone, coffee maker, personalised mini bar, hair dryer, in-room safe, iron & ironing board, iPod docking station and nightly turn down service. The original rooms (Atrium Rooms and Beachfront Suites) are located in 2-storey buildings and the newer Verandah rooms are located in 3-storey buildings. Couples Swept Away offers seven different types of suites, each with distinctive features.

Bar Services at Couples Swept Away

There are 8 bars on property offering unlimited Premium brand drinks, which are available at the Main Bar, Piano Bar for cocktails, Veggie Bar, 2 Beach Bars, a Swim-up Pool Bar, Patois Bar and a Sports Bar.

Dining at Couples Swept Away

With a selection of restaurants & grills in addition to 24-hour food service, this resort caters for all appetites.

  • The Palms: Offering a host of international and local dishes in an open-air setting.
  • Lemon Grass: Situated on the Sunset Deck overlooking the gardens, main pool and ocean, offering Authentic Thai Cuisine.
  • Feathers: The ultimate dining experience. Negril’s finest gourmet restaurant offers continental cuisine with distinctly Caribbean flair, by reservation, dress code applies.
  • Patois Patio: A relaxed and tranquil dining experience, this restaurant overlooks the pool with swim-up bar and offers ocean views and a fusion of Caribbean and Latin flavoured dishes for dinner.

Entertainment at Couples Swept Away

There is nightly entertainment from the resident band and a variety of local artists, plus a weekly Caribbean Beach party. There is also a Piano Bar and the Aura nightclub on property. Among the host of entertainment choices the dance classes, beach bonfire, piano sing-alongs, and massage class are all-time favourites.

Activities at Couples Swept Away

Featuring a 10-acre sports complex including 10 tennis courts all lit for night play (5 hard, 5 clay), with professional instruction included, 2 air-conditioned squash courts, 2 racquetball courts, basketball court, fully equipped gym with the latest Cybex equipment, 7-lane lap pool, aerobics centre, aquacise, yoga, step aerobics, ½ mile outdoor jogging track, steam rooms, saunas and a Jacuzzi are available at no extra cost. Unlimited golf is offered at the nearby Negril Hills 18- hole golf course with free transportation and green fees (mandatory caddy at extra charge, clubs and carts available for hire).

Water sports include scuba diving (PADI 5 star facility), water-skiing, sailing, windsurfing, snorkelling, kayaks, and glass bottom boat trips. All include professional instruction.

Scuba diving: Dives are offered Sun-Fri with a deep dive in the morning and shallow dive in the afternoon. A two tank dive is available Sat mornings but no afternoon dive is available. Dives and gear are included in the price of your stay; certified divers can dive once per day, subject to availability. For non-certified divers and beginners, Resort certification is available and can be arranged through the dive shop and includes one free dive, offered Mon-Fri. PADI certification, Advanced and Specialty Dives can be organised at an additional charge. Guests must sign up at least one day in advance.

In addition to the above facilities there is an indoor games room with board games, pool, table tennis and darts plus a satellite television. There are two Jacuzzis situated at either end of the large freshwater swimming pool with swim up bar. A further two Jacuzzis can be found in the gardens.

Excursions at Couples Swept Away

Couples Swept Away offers a Sunset Catamaran Cruise, a trip to a popular Sunset Waterside bar, shopping shuttle to Negril and a trip to Margaritaville. All excursions must be reserved at least 24 hours in advance, subject to availability.

  • Dolphin Cove Negril
  • Black River Safari & Y.S. Falls Tour
  • Canopy Zipline Gliding Tour
  • Horseback Ride N'Swim
  • Mayfield Falls

Couples Swept Away Spa

It’s intimate engaging with world-class treatments and tropical specialities. There are eight private treatment rooms aligned with a long Buddha plunge pool. The treatments are all designed to balance your mind and body and leave you feeling fully physically refreshed. From massage to seaweed treatments, beauty therapy to body wraps, everything you can imagine from a top-class, award-winning spa is here – and rather a lot more.
