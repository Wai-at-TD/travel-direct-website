Coconut Bay

Set amongst white sand and swaying palm trees, Coconut Bay is postcard-perfect Caribbean property. The all-inclusive resort spans more than 85 acres to include 250 rooms, six restaurants, seven bars, five pools, St. Lucia’s largest water park and much more. Facilities are split across two wings Harmony, strictly for adults, and Splash, specially designed for families.

The large resort sits on St. Lucia’s south-eastern shore, just a few-minutes drive from Maria Islands Nature Reserve and Hewanorra International Airport. The capital city of Castries is approximately an hour and a half away.

Bar Services at Coconut Bay

Coconut Bay is home to seven different bars dotted throughout the resort, serving up a wide selection of premium liquors, local beer and soft drinks. Make sure to ask about the signature “Cocktail of the Day”.

  • Lobby Bar: An open-air bar with a gazebo, fire pits and lively atmosphere.
  • Harmony Pool Bar: This adults-only venue boasts some of the most entertaining bartenders on the island. The friendly mixologists regale guests with local stories and songs.
  • Blu Bar: An open-air sports bar; the perfect place to watch the big game over a cool beer.
  • Swim-Up Pool Bar: Guests can enjoy refreshing drinks without ever leaving the pool.
  • Peter John’s Lounge: A sophisticated spot featuring live entertainment and professional DJs. The menu includes a special Rums of the Caribbean range, Coconut Bay’s collection of the region’s finest rums.
  • Splash Bar: A vibrant entertainment spot that conjures the spirit of St. Lucia with live Caribbean music and dancing.
  • Kids Swim-up Bar: Exclusively for children, this fun swim-up bar serves mocktails and smoothies.
  • Uncommon Grind: A self-service location where guests can grab some tea or coffee.

Dining at Coconut Bay

Boasting six different restaurants, guests can find a dish, view and atmosphere for every occasion.

  • Silk: This elegant dinner spot features a pan-Asian menu made with fresh, local ingredients. Reservations must be made in advance.
  • Calabash: A fine dining restaurant with one of the largest wine cellars in S. Lucia. Its varied menu features dishes from all over the world, including a few local favourites. Reservations must be made in advance.
  • Coconut Walk Marketplace: Open for breakfast, lunch and dinner, this is a buffet-style restaurant where guests can feast on international treats.
  • Capri: An open-air eatery serving up fresh Italian cuisine. The artisanal pizzas are particularly popular. Reservations required.
  • Beach Grill: Pulled pork, hand-tossed pizza and jerk chicken wings are the specialties of this casual grill. Guests are invited for lunch, dinner and late-night snacks.
  • La Luna: This intimate oceanfront restaurant is ideal for special occasion dining. Supplemental cost applies.

Entertainment at Coconut Bay

The lively resort hosts nightly concerts and an exciting rotation of special theme-dinners. Caribbean Night provides a glimpse into the rich culture of the islands while Country and Western Night is a fun taste of the American West. Karaoke takes place twice a week and professional entertainers visit the resort regularly.

Activities at Coconut Bay

Coconut Bay treats guests to a daily schedule of recreational activities. Beach volleyball, yoga, water polo, cricket, snorkelling, reggae dance classes and much more are on offer. Thrill-seekers should make a bee-line for the Surf Shack, where beginners and pros alike can practise their kitesurfing skills.

Excursions at Coconut Bay

The luxury resort offers a diverse range exciting excursions to help guests experience St. Lucia. Trips include Segway tours, whale watching, zip-lining, deep-sea fishing, village barbeques, scuba diving and much more. These are not part of the all-inclusive package.

Coconut Bay Spa

Coconut Bay’s eight-room spa offers a deluxe range of treatments designed to relax and restore. The outdoor cabanas are idyllic spots for vanilla coco massage, sea-salt scrub or vitamin-c facial. Many of the body treatments use organic ingredients indigenous to the region.

Weddings at Coconut Bay

The natural beauty of Coconut Bay Resort makes it the perfect place to tie the knot. The wedding service offers a number of all-inclusive nuptial packages for a stress-free “I do” experience. The wide range of packages include bridal spa treatments, tropical decorations and even sunset sailing trips. Newly-hitched couples can go straight from their beach wedding to a romantic Caribbean honeymoon.
