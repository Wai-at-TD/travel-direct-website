Jewel Paradise Cove

A luxury resort focused on the health and relaxation of its guests, Jewel Paradise Cove is an oceanfront oasis of wellness. The adults-only, all-inclusive resort provides ample opportunities to enrich the mind, body and soul with its world-class spa, state-of-the-art fitness facilities and exciting menu of excursions. Seven onsite restaurants serve up dishes from all over the world while the bars offer unlimited premium drinks.

The secluded property sits on Jamaica’s idyllic north coast, just 42 miles from Montego Bay Sangster International Airport.

Rooms at Jewel Paradise Cove

The property includes 210 rooms and 15 junior suites, each equipped with a spacious patio or balcony. Luxury bedding, deluxe bathrooms and an array of modern amenities help ensure your stay is a comfortable one.

  • Premier Guest Room: Overlooks lush tropical gardens.
  • Pool View Guestroom: Features views of the pool area.
  • Ocean View Guestroom: Offers sweeping views of the hotel’s landscaped lawns and the Caribbean Sea.
  • Oceanfront Concierge Guestroom: Guests that stay in these sought-after seafront rooms are treated to a complimentary concierge service.

More room types

  • Junior Suite (with Butler Service): Plush furnishings, state-of-the-art facilities, an enhanced mini-bar and attentive butler service combine for truly luxurious experience. The elegant bathroom comes complete with a Jacuzzi-style bathtub.
  • Oceanfront Junior Suite (with Butler Service): The resort’s most desirable accommodation, the splendour of a junior suite is complemented by superb sea views.

Bar Services at Jewel Paradise Cove

The all-inclusive resort encompasses five distinct bars, so you’re bound to find a cocktail and ambiance to suit your mood. Choose from a jazz cafe, tropical disco, swim-up pool bar and more. Smoothie stations can be found throughout the hotel.

Dining at Jewel Paradise Cove

Dining options are varied and exciting at Jewel Paradise Cove, which is home to seven different restaurants.

  • Aquamarina Beach Grill: Operating with the motto, “no shirt, no shoes, quality service”, this casual eatery serves up sandwiches, burgers, salads and more.
  • Court Jesters: Thin-crust, customisable, fire-oven pizzas are the specialty of this popular snack outlet.
  • Coral Café: By day, this lively café operates as an open-air buffet but when night falls it transforms into a full-service restaurant with a la carte West Indian cuisine.
  • Golden Sabre Sushi: Plates are piled high with fresh sushi, sashimi and more.
  • Moonstone by Sophia: Guests can enjoy Italian food and wine while gazing out over the Caribbean Sea.
  • Platinum: Ideal for romantic tete-a-tetes or special occasion dining, this formal restaurant is home to fine contemporary Caribbean cuisine.
  • The Jade Samurai: Highly-trained chefs serve teppanyaki dishes with a twist. Reservations are required.

Jamaican Hospitality

Jamaica’s renowned beauty, cuisine and warmth of spirit collide in this spectacular beachfront resort. Providing the opportunity for romance, relaxation and revelry, Jewel Paradise Cove is the perfect destination to unwind.

Entertainment at Jewel Paradise Cove

Live music is a regular occurrence at this vibrant Jamaican resort, with musicians delighting guests nightly at Jasper’s Chillin’ Piano Bar and Bling Disco Bar. A poolside film screening takes place every week, accompanied with Caribbean-themed snacks.

Activities at Jewel Paradise Cove

Centred around health and wellbeing, there’s no shortage of activities at Jewel Paradise Cove. The resort features a fitness centre, tennis courts, an array classes, yoga facilities, beach volleyball courts, a fit trail, enhanced water sports activities and more. Green fees are on the house at Runaway Bay Golf Club. Jewel Paradise Cove offers tailor-made scuba diving trips and training to suit all levels of experience. The crystal-clear Caribbean waters are ideal for viewing the colourful reefs that teem with life.

Excursions at Jewel Paradise Cove

Jewel Paradise Cove offers sailing trips and glass bottom boat tours as well as a number of excursions through Jamaica Tours. Book in advance.

  • Chukka Canopy Tours: An exploration trail of decks and platforms in the trees of a forest.
  • Cool Runnings Cruise to Dunn’s River Falls: Jamaica’s number one attraction combined with water sports.
  • Dolphin Cove Experience: An opportunity to interact with friendly dolphins.
  • Dunn’s River Falls: A trip to the famous waterfalls.
  • Mystic Mountain – Zip Line & Sky Explorer: A thrilling zip line ride through lush forest.

More excursions

  • Mystic Mountain Bobsled: An 100 m ride through tropical landscapes in a custom-designed bobsled.
  • Ochos Rios Highlights: A trip to the town of Ochos Rios followed by a duty-free shopping centre, Fern Gully, Coyaba Botanical Gardens and Dunns River Falls.
  • Spirit of Reggae: A visit to the village of Nine Miles, where reggae legend Bob Marley was born and laid to rest.

Jewel Paradise Cove Spa

The resort’s oceanfront spa offers a varied menu of wellness and beauty services, including massages, body wraps, facials, and meditation sessions. Treatments are provided by highly-trained specialists.

Weddings at Jewel Paradise Cove

Couples looking to tie the knot can choose from six comprehensive wedding packages. You and your loved one can say your vows on the pier, in a wedding gazebo or with your feet in the soft sand of a private beach. All packages include a personal wedding consultant for a hassle-free “I do” experience.
