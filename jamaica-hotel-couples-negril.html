Couples Negril

The ultimate all-inclusive holiday experience, exclusively for couples. Guests enjoy fine dining and unlimited premium drinks from the resort’s international restaurants and lively bars. Scuba, snorkelling, volleyball, tennis and more take place on the resort’s idyllic grounds, filled with swaying coconut trees and tropical flowers. Couples over 18 years only.

Couples Negril sits on 18 acres of tropical gardens, facing 1,000 feet of white sand beach and the blue waters of the Caribbean Sea. The laidback fishing village of Negril is just 5 miles away and Montego Bay airport is 55 miles east.

Rooms at Couples Negril

Couples Negril is home to 234 beautifully-appointed guestrooms spread throughout nine buildings. Each room is fully equipped with air conditioning, tea and coffee-making facilities, a mini bar, salon-style hair dryers and iPod and MP3 connectivity. Large French doors open on to private balconies or terraces.

  • Deluxe Garden: located throughout the property with views of the beautifully landscaped gardens.
  • Deluxe Ocean: Located throughout the property with partial ocean views.
  • Deluxe Beachfront: Located in the blocks closest to the ocean with partial ocean views.
  • Garden Suite: In addition each suite has a private oversized bathroom with double vanity, Jacuzzi bathtub & separate shower, sitting area, bathrobes and a hammock on the patio/balcony.
  • Beachfront Suite: Same inclusions as per the Garden Suite but located at the ends of each block, closest to the ocean with beautiful views of the Caribbean Sea.

Bar Services at Couples Negril

Embracing the festive spirit of Jamaica, Couples Negril boasts four sublime bars. Their expert mixologists serve up a menu of premium drinks, including cocktails, wines, beers, smoothies, coffees and much more. Beach waiters deliver tropical treats directly to sun loungers.

  • Swim-Up Pool Bar: Guests can order and enjoy a refreshing beverage without ever leaving the pool. The tiki hut provides dry seating.
  • Beach Bar: A shady spot set right on the sand.
  • The Terrace Bar: This open-air patio plays host to live entertainment every evening.
  • Piano Bar: Refinement meets revelry as the resident pianist plays requests in this popular hangout.

Dining at Couples Negril

Couples Negril is home to three specialty restaurants along with a casual beachside grill, offering expertly-prepared dishes from around the world. Complimentary morning room service provides guests with breakfast in bed.

  • Cassava Terrace: Local and international dishes are served in a relaxed yet refined environment.
  • Beach Grill & Heliconia: Grilled meat, salads and Jamaican classics are served throughout the day while dinner features Mediterranean specialties.
  • Lychee: The open-air kitchen allows guests to watch the restaurant’s expert chefs prepare Asian fusion cuisine.
  • Otaheite: An elegant eatery serving up authentic Jamaican fare.

Entertainment at Couples Negril

Life’s a party at Couples Negril, where guests can always find somewhere to let their hair down. On Mondays, the night kicks off at the manager’s cocktail party before moving down to the beach for a bonfire and gentle acoustic music. Tuesday sees the legendary cheese and wine soiree while Fridays include a ritual toast to the sunset on the boardwalk. A party is held on the beach every evening.

Activities at Couples Negril

This self-described “beachfront playground” offers a wealth of ways to spend the day including top-notch health and wellbeing classes and recreational activities. Adventurous types may prefer to get their work out on the water, with paddle boarding, water skiing and more available, while competitive couples will enjoy a game of volleyball, and tennis. Those looking to exercise their mind and spirit can join a meditation session or relaxing morning nature stroll.

Excursions at Couples Negril

Couples Negril is eager to showcase the beauty and culture of Jamaica. Its specially-crafted excursions help guests to experience the wealth of delights the island has to offer. Excursions include:

  • Glass-bottom boat trip
  • Guided shopping tour
  • Golf at Negril Hills
  • Catamaran cruise

Couples Negril Spa

Couples Negril offers a wide array of deluxe spa services performed in idyllic and quirky treatment rooms. Guests can enjoy a seaweed body wrap in a thatched beach hut, a reflexology session in a tree house and much more. Many spa therapies and beauty services use organic ingredients native to the Caribbean.

Weddings at Couples Negril

Situated in one of the most romantic location imaginable, Couples Negril is the perfect place to tie the knot. The resort boasts an experienced team of professional wedding planners who will take care of all the necessary nuptial details. You can say your vows in one of the resort’s lush gardens or its spectacular stretch of sandy beach.
