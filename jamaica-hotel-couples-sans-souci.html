Couples Sans Souci

Boasting a number of accreditations, Couples Sans Souci fits perfectly within the Couples Resorts brand portfolio. This idyllic property is perfect for Couples wanting to relax and enjoy the many all inclusive features including 24-hour food service, unlimited international premium brand drinks and a variety of land and watersports plus organised off property excursions. Couples only over 18 years.
Watch the resort video and see for yourself. The low key, lively atmosphere at Couples Sans Souci adds to its popularity as a destination for couples’ holidays. The beachfront location is the focal point of water based activities during the day and by night couples can relax and warm up by a late night beach bonfire under the stars.

A private 96-acre island hideaway separated from the Andros mainland by a turquoise ocean inlet, Couples Resorts is an oasis of laidback hospitality, lush tropical foliage and deserted white sand beaches. Located in Ocho Rios, a one-time fishing village now one of Jamaica's most popular destinations, thanks to a postcard-perfect northern coastline, arts, crafts and superior shopping. Must-see sights range from neearby Bunn's River Falls to the Ocho Rios Jerk Center, home to spicy, island-style smoked jerk meats. If the setting seems familiar, you may have seen it on film. Parts of the first James Bond movie, Dr. No, were filmed here.

Rooms at Couples Sans Souci

Accommodation comprises no fewer than 150 luxury suites that feature French or sliding doors onto fully furnished, sun-kissed and private balconies.

  • Deluxe Ocean: comfortable sitting area looking out to sea, without balcony / patio.
  • Deluxe Ocean Verandah: Junior Suites with sitting area. Some with spacious walk-in showers.
  • One Bedroom Ocean Suite with Balcony: Suites with separate living room, balcony and sleeping area. Some with spacious walk-in showers.
  • One Bedroom Beachfront Suite: Suites with separate living and sleeping area, bathroom with Jacuzzi bathtub and stand-up shower, balcony or patio.
  • Penthouse Suites: Suites with living rooms, elegant bathrooms, king size beds and large balconies.
  • Hibiscus Cottage: 1 Cottage, featuring private outdoor Jacuzzi, spacious living room with flat screen TV, large master bedroom and bathroom suite with shower and Jacuzzi bathtub for two. There is also a separate room which may be used for private en-suite massage treatments if desired.

Bar Services at Couples Sans Souci

Six bars located throughout the resort offering premium internationally branded drinks from 10.00 through until 02.00, Charlie’s Spa Bar for natural health juices and a Swim-up bar.

Dining at Couples Sans Souci

With a selection of restaurants & grills in addition to 24-hour food service, this resort caters for all appetites.

Entertainment at Couples Sans Souci

Jamaica’s finest musicians & singers will entertain you throughout your stay with a variety of acts including live music, shows, wandering musicians and a weekly beach party.

Activities at Couples Sans Souci

Land sports and activities include 2 hard tennis courts (lit for night play) available with complimentary instruction, beach volleyball and unlimited golf at the Upton Hills 18-hole golf course with free transportation and green fees (mandatory caddy at extra charge, clubs and carts available for hire). There is also a fitness centre with fully equipped gym and aerobics studio for classes including yoga, aerobics, pilates and aquacise. In addition there are 4 swimming pools including a mineral water pool and three jacuzzis, an indoor games room with various games and table tennis.

Water sports include scuba diving (PADI 5 star facility), water-skiing, sailing, windsurfing, snorkelling, kayaks, and glass bottom boat trips. All include professional instruction.

Scuba diving: Dives are offered Sun-Fri with a deep dive in the morning and shallow dive in the afternoon. A two tank dive is available Sat mornings but no afternoon dive is available. Dives and gear are included in the price of your stay; certified divers can dive once per day, subject to availability. For non-certified divers and beginners, Resort certification is available and can be arranged through the dive shop and includes one free dive, offered Mon-Fri. PADI certification, Advanced and Specialty Dives can be organised at an additional charge. Guests must sign up at least one day in advance.

In addition to the above facilities there is an indoor games room with board games, pool, table tennis and darts plus a satellite television. There are two Jacuzzis situated at either end of the large freshwater swimming pool with swim up bar. A further two Jacuzzis can be found in the gardens.

Excursions at Couples Sans Souci

Couples Resorts offer guests the opportunity to explore Jamaica and make new friends with a selection of trips. All tours are subject to availability and must be booked at least 24 hours in advance.

  • A spectacular tour of Dunn’s River Falls
  • Shopping trip to Ocho Rios
  • Trip to the famous Margaritaville Bar every Monday.

Couples Sans Souci Spa

Charlie’s Spa has earned praise as one of the best Spa in all the Caribbean. Named after its friendly resident turtle, Charlie who lived for more than 100 years old in the mineral spring grotto that bubbles up from beneath the resort with rejuvenating waters that rival the great natural mineral springs in Europe. Spa treatments include massage, body scrubs, facials and other treatments to relax and rejuvenate. All spa and beauty treatments are at extra cost payable locally and must be reserved in advance.

Indulge in the luxury of your very own garden wedding at Couples Resorts Sans Souci. Host your closest friends and family for an unforgettably romantic ceremony in our wedding gazebo, followed by a classic Jamaican rum punch toast. Savor a taste of the islands with a delectable designer wedding cake or cupcakes adorned with tropical flowers, accented by gazebo decorations and fresh tropical flowers.
