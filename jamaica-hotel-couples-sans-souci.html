Couples Sans Souci

Set amongst verdant mountains, sandy beaches, tropical gardens and turquoise waters, this luxury couples-only resort is a heavenly spot to spend time with the one you love. Couples San Souci is home to 150 suites, three restaurants, six bars, four swimming pools and a stunning stretch of beach. Couples must be aged 18 and over.
Watch the resort video and see for yourself. The low key, lively atmosphere at Couples Sans Souci adds to its popularity as a destination for couples’ holidays. The beachfront location is the focal point of water based activities during the day and by night couples can relax and warm up by a late night beach bonfire under the stars.

Couples San Souci sits on a private 96-acre island, separated from the mainland by an ocean inlet. The nearest town is Ocho Rios, a one-time fishing village now one of Jamaica’s most popular destinations thanks to its picturesque coastline and superb shopping destinations.

Rooms at Couples Sans Souci

Couples San Souci’s luxury suites come equipped with all the facilities you’d expect from a five-star hotel, including air conditioning, coffee makers, salon-style hair dryers, flat screen TVs with satellite channels and an iPod or MP3 player. Furnishings feature calming blue hues and the French or sliding doors open onto fully-furnished balconies.

  • Deluxe Ocean: Comfortable sitting area looking out to sea. French doors lead to an enclosed covered seating area.
  • Deluxe Ocean Verandah: Junior Suites with sitting area. Some with spacious walk-in showers. Enjoys views of the ocean.
  • One Bedroom Ocean Suite: Suites with separate living room, balcony and sleeping area. Some with spacious walk-in showers. Situated by the natural spring mineral pool, they feature stunning views of the Caribbean.
  • One Bedroom Beachfront Suite: Suites with separate living and sleeping area, bathroom with Jacuzzi bathtub and stand-up shower, balcony or patio. Overlook an immaculate lawn and the beach.
  • Penthouse Suites: Suites with living rooms, elegant bathrooms, king size beds and large balconies. Enjoy spectacular views of the sea.
  • Hibiscus Cottage: 1 Cottage, featuring private outdoor Jacuzzi, spacious living room with flat screen TV, large master bedroom and bathroom suite with shower and Jacuzzi bathtub for two. There is also a separate room which may be used for private en-suite massage treatments if desired.

Bar Services at Couples Sans Souci

Couples San Souci’s six bars offer everything from vitamin-packed smoothies to truly decadent cocktails. The beach waiter service allows guests to order drinks without leaving their sun lounger.

  • Pool Bar: Located within the main pool, guests can swim-up to order and enjoy their drinks.
  • Charlie’s Veggie Bar: Offering an extremely healthy menu of smoothies and fresh snacks.
  • Beach Bar: A relaxed seaside venue with light reggae music and friendly staff.
  • Sunset Beach Bar: Located by the resort’s au natural sunbathing beach, this casual bar boasts an extensive drinks menu.
  • Palazzina Bar: A refined spot for a beer, wine or expertly prepared cocktail.
  • Balloon Piano Bar: An artistic space featuring a beautiful piano; sing-alongs are a regular occurrence.
  • Martini Bar: Whether you like it shaken or stirred, dry or wet, the expert mixologists will prepare a martini exactly to your tastes.

Dining at Couples Sans Souci

Couples San Souci’s three distinct restaurants provide an exquisite dining experience, offering both local and international dishes, always expertly prepared. Guests can also enjoy breakfast, lunch and dinner in the privacy of their own suite with complimentary in-room dining.

  • Palazzina: A bistro-style restaurant with dishes from around the world.
  • The Beach Grill & Bella Vista: A casual scenic spot, offering a number of Caribbean dishes.
  • Casanova: A fine dining establishment with colonial-inspired furnishings and a varied menu.

Jamaican Hospitality

Jamaica’s renowned beauty, cuisine and warmth of spirit collide in this spectacular coastal resort. Providing the opportunity for romance, relaxation and revelry, Couples San Souci is the perfect destination to unwind.

Entertainment at Couples Sans Souci

There are plenty of opportunities for mingling and merriment at Couples San Souci, where lavish parties are a regular occurrence. The week kicks off with Monday’s Manager Cocktail Party, featuring delicious poolside libations, while Thursday sees a wine-tasting party held in the incredibly refined Ballroom Bar. On Fridays, it’s the Starlight Gala and Cabaret, with live music and a party on the Au Naturel Beach. The weekly Couples Beach Party is legendary, featuring an extravagant Caribbean buffet accompanied by a performance from some of Jamaica’s finest folk artists.

Activities at Couples Sans Souci

Days can be as lazy or lively as guests like at Couples San Souci, home to outstanding sports facilities and a wealth of recreational activities. The fully-equipped fitness centre features high-tech cardio machines, free weights and a team of certified trainers. Spinning, aerobics, yoga, pilates and meditation sessions are held every day. Those looking to pick up a new skill can take a variety of classes – including massage, dance, hat making and wood carving – or try out some water sports equipment.

Excursions at Couples Sans Souci

Couples San Souci’s generous all-inclusive package extends to offsite exploration. The resort offers the following trips free of charge:

  • Glass-bottom boat trip
  • Trip to Margaritaville
  • Dunn’s River Falls
  • Shopping tour
  • Unlimited Golf at Upton Golf & Country Club

Couples Sans Souci Spa

Awarded the prestigious title of “Best Caribbean Spa” by Travel + Leisure, the San Souci spa offers stunning surroundings, incredible services and expert therapists. Their menu of treatments includes bamboo fusion massage, reflexology, hand rejuvenation treatment, apres soleil facial and much more.

Weddings at Couples Sans Souci

A destination dedicated to romance, Couples San Souci is an idyllic setting for the Caribbean wedding of your dreams. The professional staff take care of every little detail and are committed to creating dream big days. Brides and grooms can say their vows on the beach or in one the resort’s tropical gardens.
