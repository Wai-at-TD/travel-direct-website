Dover Beach Hotel

Though situated just a stone’s-throw away from one of the island’s liveliest neighbourhoods, Dover Beach Hotel is an oasis of beauty and tranquillity. The popular resort encompasses a private beach, pool and sundeck, so there are plenty of places to relax and recharge before sampling the vibrant nightlife of Saint Lawrence Gap. Originally opened in 1977, Dover Beach Hotel has become an island institution and is proud of its superb reputation amongst islanders and visitors alike.

Sitting on the scenic South Coast, Dover Beach Hotel boasts easy access to many of the country’s most popular tourist attractions. Bridgetown, Barbados’ UNESCO-listed capital city, is less than a twenty-minute drive away.

Rooms at Dover Beach Hotel

Dover Beach Hotel’s 59 rooms are spread across three buildings named Calais, Goldwater and Flowers. Calais sits directly on the beach, Flowers is set amongst tropical gardens and Goldwater stands as an island structure with its own gardens. Rooms come equipped with modern conveniences for a comfortable stay such as televisions, coffee machines, hairdryers and radios. All rooms have a balcony or terrace.

Room types

  • Standard Rooms: Located in the Goldwater Block.
  • Garden View Studios: Located in the Flowers and Calais Block.
  • Pool View Studios: Located on the ground floor of the Calais Block, guests staying in these room can walk straight from their room to the pool.
  • Ocean View Studios: Located in the Calais Block, these rooms enjoy uninterrupted views of the Caribbean Sea.
  • Garden View One Bedroom Apartment: Popular with families, these rooms can accommodate larger groups.

Bar Services at Dover Beach Hotel

With views of the pool and ocean, Dover Beach’s Pool Bar is a popular spot for drinks, snacks and meals. Breakfast, lunch and dinner are served here and a delicious happy hour takes place daily. Saint Lawrence Gap is just around the corner, home to countless rum shacks, restaurants and pubs.

AN ARRAY OF ACTIVITIES TO ENJOY

Dover Beach Hotel’s superb location grants guests easy access to a wide range of activities and entertainment. Alternatively, spend your day lazing, paddling and splashing at the private beach or in the swimming pool.

Guests can enjoy a 15% discount on all green fees at the renowned Barbados Golf Club.

Excursions at Dover Beach Hotel

With so many tourist attractions nearby, the resort is perfectly placed for guests that want to discover more about the island’s rich history and distinctive culture. The area boasts a number of museums and landmarks and the hotel’s friendly staff are happy to arrange trips and tours. Adventurous types may prefer to organise a horseback riding excursion.

Weddings at Dover Beach Hotel

Loved-up couples can enjoy the ultimate in romantic nuptials without the stress of planning them thanks to Dover Beach Hotel’s comprehensive wedding package. The service includes a wedding cake, a bottle of champagne, the bride’s bouquet and the groom’s buttonhole along with all administrative and legal fees. Steel pans, video recordings and photos are available at an extra cost.
