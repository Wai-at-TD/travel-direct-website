Couples Swept Away

Set on seven miles of pristine sandy beach, Couples Swept Away represents a true island paradise. The romantic resort, exclusively for couples aged over 18, is particularly popular with active guests due to its massive fitness complex, three swimming pools and the wide assortment of adventure activities on offer. The six restaurants and seven bars serve offer fine dining and premium drinks.
Watch the resort video and see for yourself. The low key, lively atmosphere at Couples Swept Away adds to its popularity as a destination for couples’ holidays. The beachfront location is the focal point of water based activities during the day and by night couples can relax and warm up by a late night beach bonfire under the stars.

Couples Swept Away is located within Negril, on the west coast of Jamaica. Montego Bay airport lies 60-miles away, taking an hour and a half to reach by road.

Rooms at Couples Swept Away

Couples Swept Away is home to 312 luxury rooms and suites spread throughout 27 acres of tropical landscaping. Elegant, calming and incredibly romantic, guestrooms feature cool terracotta floors, cedar shutters and French doors that open onto a private balcony or terrace. Furnishings and amenities include plush king-sized beds, air conditioning, personalised mini bars, salon-style hair dryers and iPod docking stations. Guests can enjoy stunning views, with several room types overlooking the lush tropical gardens and azure waters of the Caribbean Sea.

Bar Services at Couples Swept Away

Home to seven distinct bars, guests have a wide choice of spots to quench their thirst and soak up the warm Jamaican atmosphere. The beach bar flag service allows guests to order drinks from the comfort of their sun lounger.

  • Wellness Bar: Offers a range of healthy juices, smoothies and snacks.
  • Swim-Up Pool: Guests don’t need to leave the water to order and enjoy their tipple of choice.
  • Bamboo Bars: These quirky huts are spread throughout the main beach, so guests are never far from a cool one.
  • Main Bar: Intimate and comfortable, this central meeting spot hosts live entertainment five nights a week.
  • Patois Bar: Expert bartenders prepare colourful cocktails in this lively evening spot.
  • Martini Bar: This vibrant lounge boasts a vast list of Martini recipes along with a billiards room and regular live entertainment.
  • Aura Bar: An energetic night club with an in-house pianist and nightly DJ set.

Dining at Couples Swept Away

Whether they’re serving Jamaican delicacies or dishes from far-flung lands, Couples Swept Away’s six restaurants offer a gourmet dining experience. The professional chefs use fresh, local and sustainably-sourced ingredients wherever possible.

  • The Palms: Breakfast features fresh fruits, pastries and breads while lunch and dinner includes a rich offering of salads and treats from the depths of the Caribbean Sea.
  • Patois Patio: This colourful restaurant boasts a menu of exciting Latin and Caribbean flavours along with gorgeous ocean views.
  • Seagrape Cafe: A casual seafront spot serving juices, smoothie, sandwiches and healthy vegetable dips.
  • Cabana Grill: A popular beach grill with legendary jerk chicken.
  • Lemon Grass: An Asian-style restaurant specialising in Thai fare.
  • Feathers: The resort’s ultimate fine dining establishment, the menu includes exciting dishes such as coconut shrimp ceviche, plantain and crab meat stuffed chicken breast and passion fruit crème brule.

Jamaican Hospitality

Jamaica’s renowned beauty, cuisine and warmth of spirit collide in this spectacular coastal resort. Providing the opportunity for romance, relaxation and revelry, Couples Swept Away is the perfect destination to unwind.

Entertainment at Couples Swept Away

The entertainment lasts till late at this adults-only all-inclusive, where live music is a regular occurrence. Guests can belt it out on karaoke nights or watch the professionals at work with Jamaican steel pan performances and piano sessions. Every Monday, the manager holds a cocktail party and on Fridays there’s a party on the beach, with lots of food and live music.

Activities at Couples Swept Away

Couples Swept Away takes health, fun and fitness seriously, with 10 of its 19 acres dedicated to sports and recreation. Guests have access to state-of-the-art fitness facilities, professional grade tennis courts with complementary lessons, two squash courts, two racquetball courts, a jogging track, a full menu of classes and much more.

Taking full advantage of its stunning oceanfront setting, Couples Swept Away also offers scuba diving classes, snorkelling tours, hobie cat sailing, kayaking, water skiing, windsurfing and paddle boarding. Alternatively, guests can discover more of the estate through a guided nature walk.

Excursions at Couples Swept Away

The comprehensive all-inclusive package extends to exploration around beautiful Jamaica. Couples Swept Away provides the following trips free of charge:

  • Glass-bottom boat trip
  • Shopping tour
  • Golf a Negril Hills (complimentary green fees and transport only)
  • Catamaran cruise
  • Trip to Margaritaville (complimentary transport only)
  • Trip to Sunset Bar (complimentary transport only)

Couples Swept Away Spa

The luxury resort is home to a world-class spa, boasting an extensive treatment list with tropical twists. Therapies such as hot stone massage, seaweed body wraps and deep pore cleansing are designed to balance the mind and body, leaving the client refreshed and relaxed. The eight treatment rooms circle an invigorating plunge pool.

Weddings at Couples Swept Away

Offering a picturesque stretch of sandy beach and glorious garden gazebos, there are few better places to tie the knot than Couples Swept Away. Their professional wedding planners and romance concierges can take care of all the details – from breakfast to bouquets – leaving you to enjoy your big day.
