Calabash Cove

This boutique resort offers a luxury all-inclusive experience in a gorgeous beachfront setting. Every building of the resort has been designed to sit in harmony with the bay’s natural environment. The lobby, restaurant, bar, sanctuary and pool lie on a lush slope overlooking the Caribbean Sea, perfect for catching one of Saint Lucia’s renowned sunsets.

Calabash Cove sits on a palm tree-lined beach in the north west of the island. Though the resort feels secluded, Rodney Bay Marina is just 3.7 miles away and the capital city of Castries can be reached in under 15 minutes.

Rooms, Services, and Facilities. Decidedly Accommodating

Calabash Cove

Rooms at Calabash Cove

Accommodation is divided into three equally luxurious categories. All rooms have king-sized beds, Jacuzzis, showers, mini bars, large flat-screen TVs, high speed internet access and air conditioning. The fully-furnished balconies and patios boast superb views of the surrounding area.

  • Sunset Ocean View Junior Suite: Luxury suites offering stunning ocean vistas. Guests can sip a glass of wine on their private patio while admiring the sunset. 620-660 square feet.
  • Swim-Up Junior Site: Perched on the edge of the infinity pool, guests can swim directly from their room to the pool bar. 620 square feet.
  • Waters Edge Cottage: Beachfront cottages with complete living rooms and panoramic views of Bonaire Bay. Guests can take a dip in their private plunge pool or enjoy an outdoor rain shower. 1000 square feet.

Bar Services at Calabash Cove

The elegantly furnished C Bar overlooks the Caribbean Sea, making it the perfect spot for a sunset aperitif. Cocktails are made with premium-brand liquors and the adjacent wine cellar stocks a variety of international wines.

The swim-up pool bar boasts a comprehensive drinks, snacks and lunch menu. There’s an accompanying deck for guests that don’t want to get wet.

Dining at Calabash Cove

The resort’s Windsong Restaurant serves up an exciting menu of Caribbean and international cuisine, including a number of Asian dishes. The chefs use local, fresh ingredients wherever possible and guests are encouraged to sample local delicacies, such as St Lucian crab. Open for breakfast, lunch and dinner.

Saint Lucian Hospitality

St Lucia’s renowned beauty and warmth of spirit collide in this spectacular beachfront resort. Providing the opportunity for romance, relaxation and revelry, Calabash Cove is the perfect destination to unwind.

Entertainment at Calabash Cove

Local entertainers perform at Windsong Restaurant every evening, from 7pm till 9pm.

Activities at Calabash Cove

Guests can spend their days swimming in the pool, strolling through the colourful gardens or unwinding on the beach. The Whispers room has been designed as an oasis of calm and relaxation, where guests can kick back with their favourite book, listen to music or do absolutely nothing at all. Sporty types can keep up their fitness regime with the resort’s state-of-the-art gym.

Excursions at Calabash Cove

Calabash Cove provides a leisure concierge service that organises individually-tailored trips around Saint Lucia. Excursions include tours of Castries, Pigeon Island National Historic Park and Mount Pimbard.

Calabash Cove Spa

Ti Spa offers a wide variety of treatments to enjoy on the beach boardwalk, the seafront gazebo or in the privacy of your own room. Many of the treatments use natural plant-based products, including some grown and developed on the resort itself. Massages, scrubs, facials and manicures are just some of the services on offer.

Weddings at Calabash Cove

Couples can experience a dream Caribbean wedding at Calabash Cove. The resort offers a number of comprehensive wedding packages with the option to tie the knot on a picturesque pier, oceanfront gazebo or on the beach itself. The dedicated wedding team helps to tailor both ceremonies and receptions to guests’ specific preferences, so all you have to do is relax and look forward to the perfect big day.
