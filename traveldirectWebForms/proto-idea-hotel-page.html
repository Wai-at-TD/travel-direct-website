Hilton Rose Hall Resort & Spa

Set directly on the Caribbean Sea, Hilton Rose Hall Resort & Spa is situated along the beautiful shores of Montego Bay, on what was once the site of an 18th century sugar plantation. Discover the pleasures of this island retreat, as you immerse yourself in the warm hospitality and irresistible charm of Jamaica.
Watch the video and see for yourself. The spacious grounds of Hilton Rose Resort play host to a wide variety of activities, along with some of the most stunning scenery in the Caribbean. The luxury spa and picturesque beach are perfect places to unwind after a morning of high-adrenaline water sports.

Situated on 400 acres in beautiful Montego Bay, which is famous for its white sand beaches. Also located just seven miles from Sangster International Airport.

Rooms at Hilton Rose Hall

Our comfortable and contemporary guest rooms feature a variety of spectacular views, such as oceanfront, and thoughtful amenities, each with private balcony.

  • Resort View: Situated amongst landscaped courtyards and lawns.
  • Partial Ocean View: Showcasing the resort’s oceanfront surroundings, including the world-class Cinnamon Hill Golf Course or the majestic Jamaican Blue Mountains.
  • Ocean Front: Unforgettable views of the crystal blue Caribbean Sea from your private balcony.
  • Caribbean Suite: Contemporary and thoughtfully appointed suites consist of a spacious master bedroom, a parlor with bathroom, and a private balcony overlooking the stunning Caribbean Sea.
