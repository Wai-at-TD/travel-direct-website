Hilton Rose Hall Resort & Spa

Overlooking the Caribbean Sea on the site of an 18th century sugar plantation, Hilton Rose Hall provides the perfect holiday cocktail of culture, charm and beauty. Though it boasts a historical location, the five-star accommodation and facilities are modern, equipped with state-of-the-art technologies. Guests have access to six restaurants and two bars as part of the all-inclusive package.
Watch the video and see for yourself. The spacious grounds of Hilton Rose Resort play host to a wide variety of activities, along with some of the most stunning scenery in the Caribbean. The luxury spa and picturesque beach are perfect places to unwind after a morning of high-adrenaline water sports.

Hilton Rose Hall sits on 400 stunning acres in Montego Bay, an area famed for white sand beaches. Sangster International Airport is just seven miles away.

Rooms, Services, and Facilities. Decidedly Accommodating

Hilton Rose Hall

Rooms at Hilton Rose Hall

Guestrooms are comfortable and contemporary, equipped with thoughtful amenities. Guests enjoy luxury bedding, mini fridges, coffee makers, satellite televisions, air conditioning and high-speed internet access. Each room has its own private balcony, featuring picturesque views.

  • Resort View: Available with one king bed or two queen beds. Situated amongst landscaped courtyards and lawns. 370 square feet.
  • Partial Ocean View: Available with one king bed or two queen beds. The view showcases the resort’s oceanfront surroundings, including the world-class Cinnamon Hill Golf Course or the majestic Jamaican Blue Mountains. 370 square feet.
  • Ocean Front: Available with one king bed or two queen beds. Features unforgettable views of the Caribbean Sea from the balcony. 370 square feet.
  • Caribbean Royal Suite: Contemporary and thoughtfully appointed suites consist of a spacious master bedroom, a parlour with bathroom, and a private balcony overlooking the stunning Caribbean Sea. 1,110 square feet.

Bar Services at Hilton Rose Hall

Hilton Rose Hall is home to two popular bars staffed by expert bartenders. Guests can choose from a wide array of tasty tipples, including beers, wines, cocktails and coffees.

  • Bar King Frog: An all-day hot spot with outdoor seating, the perfect place for the resort’s signature rum cocktails.
  • The Cricket Club: An indoor late-night option with pool tables, karaoke evenings and live music entertainment.

Dining at Hilton Rose Hall

The all-inclusive resort’s six restaurants offer exceptional world cuisine, prepared by an award-winning culinary team and served by friendly wait staff.

  • Fresh: A casual buffet of international cuisine overlooking the Caribbean Sea.
  • Luna di Mare: A fine dining restaurant with a sumptuous menu of Northern Italian cuisine and an extensive wine list.
  • Three Palms Restaurant: A gourmet eatery with superb views of the golf course. Executive Chef Michael Dannecker has crafted a creative menu.
  • Moonstone: An Italian restaurant serving up Mediterranean classics such as pizza, pasta and flatbreads.
  • Seaside Bar & Grille: A laidback grill with a menu of Caribbean-inspired dishes.
  • Mangoes: An outdoor lunch spot offering salads, burgers and sandwiches.

Jamaican Hospitality

Jamaica’s renowned beauty, cuisine and warmth of spirit collide in this spectacular coastal resort. Providing the opportunity for romance, relaxation and revelry, Hilton Rose Hall is the perfect destination to unwind.

Entertainment at Hilton Rose Hall

The lively atmosphere of this Caribbean resort lasts well into the evening with a variety of music performances and shows. There’s a disco every evening at the Cricket Club, featuring a DJ, and karaoke nights take place regularly. Local musicians also visit the resort, delighting guests with authentic reggae, ska and calypso tunes.

AN ARRAY OF ACTIVITIES TO ENJOY WITHIN THIS ALL-INCLUSIVE RESORT

Spanning 400 acres, Hilton Rose Hall plays host to a wide array sports and recreational facilities, including a championship golf course, six tennis courts, a large swimming pool and 1,800 square feet of private beach. As part of the all-inclusive package, guests enjoy unlimited access to the fitness centre and complimentary use of non-motorized water sports equipment.

The Kids Klub runs daily supervised activities for children while the Teen Zone is a fun spot for guests aged 13-17 to hang out. The onsite water park – the second largest in the Caribbean -- is popular with the young and young at heart, featuring a 280-foot slide, jungle play garden, lazy river and more.

Excursions at Hilton Rose Hall

Hilton Rose Hall is perfectly positioned for further exploration around Jamaica, with a number of top tourist attractions sitting within easy reach. The resort offers exciting guided excursions through its partners Jamaica Tours Limited, Chukka Caribbean Adventures and Dreamer Catamaran Cruises. Trips include horseback riding, zip lining, sailing and more.

Hilton Rose Hall Spa

Soothe Spa is a luxury oasis of calm and indulgence. Its extensive menu of wellness and beauty treatments has been custom designed with indigenous elements from the local environment. An 18th-century aqueduct, used to bring water to the sugarcane plantation, now serves as an open-air treatment room.

Weddings at Hilton Rose Hall

Offering a wide array of romantic, atmospheric and fun venues, Hilton Rose Hall is a dream wedding location. Couples have a wide choice of settings for their vows, including the beach, waterpark, historical plantation house, pool deck and gardens. The resort’s professional wedding staff will handle all the paperwork and arrangements, including an officiant, so you can relax and focus on enjoying your big day.
