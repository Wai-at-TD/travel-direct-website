Hyatt Ziva Rose Hall

Designed for all-ages fun, Hyatt Ziva Rose Hall is the ideal destination for a family escape. The all-inclusive resort occupies the same grounds as Hyatt Zilara Rose Hall but, unlike the Zilara property welcomes children and includes superb kid-friendly facilities. The beachfront grounds encompass 387 guestrooms, seven restaurants and four bars. Much of the resort enjoys spectacular views of the Caribbean Sea and Jamaican Blue Mountains.
Watch the video and see for yourself. Hyatt Ziva Rose Hall provides fun for the whole family with its excellent child-friend facilities, superb restaurants and welcoming staff. All this is complemented by postcard-perfect scenery, unmistakably Jamaica.

Hyatt Ziva Rose Hall sits on Jamaica’s scenic north coast, in Montego Bay. The resort is just 15 minutes away from Sangster’s International Airport and the region’s top tourism attractions are within easy reach.

Rooms at Hyatt Ziva Rose Hall

Hyatt Ziva’s is home to 387 rooms and suites, featuring contemporary facilities with a distinctly Caribbean charm. All accommodation comes equipped with luxury beds, modern bathrooms and fully-furnished balconies or patios. State-of-the-art electronics are provided, including an LCD satellite television and MP3 docking station. A fully-stocked mini bar and 24-hour room service is part of the all-inclusive package.

  • Standard: Available with a king or two double beds. Terrace offers views of the swimming pool or gardens. 493 square feet.
  • Resort View: Available with a king or two double beds. Terrace overlooks the lush resort gardens. 493 square feet.
  • Deluxe Resort View: Available with a king or two double beds. Terrace overlooks lush resort gardens. 493 square feet.
  • Ocean View: Available with a king or two double beds. Features views of the Caribbean Sea. 493 square feet.
  • Ocean Front: Features two double beds. Boasts sweeping views of the Caribbean Sea. 493 square feet.
  • Swim-Up: Available with a king or two double beds. Provides direct access to the swimming pool and views of the gardens. 492 square feet. (Guests must be 13+)
  • Ocean View Junior Suite: Available with a king or two double beds. The private balcony overlooks the Caribbean Sea. 656 square feet.
  • Ocean Front Junior Suite: Available with a king or two double beds. Boasts sweeping views of the Caribbean Sea. 656 square feet.
    • Suites include an enhanced bathroom, with rainfall shower and separate tub, and a spacious seating area.
  • Ocean View Butler Junior Suite: Features a king-sized bed. Terrace overlooks the Caribbean Sea. 1,030 square feet.
  • One Bedroom Ocean View Butler Suite: Features a separate bedroom with an ocean-facing king-sized bed. Terrace overlooks the Caribbean Sea. 1,030 square feet.
  • One Bedroom Ocean View Swim-Up Butler Suite: Features a separate bedroom with a king-sized bed. Provides direct access to the swimming pool along with views of the Caribbean Sea. 1,083 square feet.
  • One Bedroom Ocean Front Butler Suite: Features a separate bedroom with an ocean-facing king-sized bed. Boasts sweeping views of the Caribbean Sea. 1,083 square feet.
    • Guests staying in Butler Suites enjoy personalised beach butler and concierge services.

  • Presidential Suite: A penthouse suite featuring a separate bedroom, whirlpool tub for two, dressing room, living room and dining room. Boasts sweeping views of the Caribbean Sea. 2,500 square feet.
    • Guests staying in Club Rooms have access to a range of additional services and perks, including private check-in and check-out, an exclusive lounge, premium liquors, complimentary Wi-Fi, beach butler and concierge services and much more.

Bar Services at Hyatt Ziva Rose Hall

Hyatt Ziva is home to a coffee shop and four different bars, where guests can unwind, mingle and sip expertly-crafted cocktails. Rum sommeliers are on hand to help you choose your perfect Jamaican tipple.

  • Fez: This intimate rooftop bar has a colourful Moroccan theme.
  • Lagoonz: Draft beer, wine and signature cocktails can all be enjoyed without ever leaving the pool at this popular swim-up bar.
  • Shakerz: An open-air bar specialising in Caribbean cocktails.
  • Stirz: Set in the hotel lobby, guests can enjoy a wide variety of beverages while relaxing on plush sofas and armchairs.
  • Grindz: Fine Jamaican coffee is served with pastries and snacks.

Dining at Hyatt Ziva Rose Hall

Hyatt Ziva offers a diverse range dining options, featuring buffet and a la carte eateries from far flung corners of the globe. As well as several gourmet restaurants, guests can satisfy their cravings at food carts dotted throughout the resort.

  • Barefoot Jerkz: A traditional jerk shack located on the beach.
  • Bitez: Deli-style sandwiches are made with fine meats, cheeses and vegetables.
  • Brazil: A Brazilian-style steak house with renowned beef skewers.
  • Calypzo: An open-air restaurant with a menu of Caribbean treats and specialty daiquiri bar.
  • Choicez: This diverse international restaurant boasts three cooking stations, where guests can watch their meal being expertly prepared.
  • Di Roza: Clay-oven pizzas and fine pasta dishes are the staples of this Italian trattoria.
  • Fuzion: A delicious taste of the East, the Fuzion menu features a diverse assortment of Asian cuisine, including Chinese wok and Japanese yakitori.
  • Union Jack’z: A sports pub serving up traditional British favourites.

Entertainment at Hyatt Ziva Rose Hall

When night falls, the party gets going at Hyatt Ziva. The all-inclusive resort provides guests with a wealth of evening entertainment options, ranging from a Michael Jackson tribute act to a steel pan performance by local musicians. The talent show gives guests a chance to show off their special skills and win prizes. Request a calendar of events upon arrival to make sure you don’t miss out.

Activities at Hyatt Ziva Rose Hall

Top-notch facilities are complemented by a jam-packed schedule of activities to ensure guests are never bored. Various events are held at the swimming pool, fitness centre and game room, such as water polo matches, yoga classes and pool tournaments. Guests can try a number of water sports on the beach or simply chill out and enjoy the scenery. A complimentary round of golf at the White Witch or Cinnamon Golf Course is included as part of the package.

The KidZ Club keeps Hyatt Ziva’s youngest guests entertained with arts and crafts, parties and games.

Excursions at Hyatt Ziva Rose Hall

Guests can discover more about the culture, history and environment of Jamaica through a wide variety of excursions. The resort’s chosen tour operator, Island Routes, offers exciting trips, ranging from plantation tours to zip lining adventures. There’s also a car rental desk for guests who want to explore the island independently.

Hyatt Ziva Rose Hall Spa

Set on the shores of the Caribbean Sea, the onsite spa provides the ultimate in relaxation and indulgence. The highly-trained staff offer a wide variety of treatments, including massages, body wraps, facial and aromatherapy. There’s even a specially-adapted facial and massage package for teenagers.

The spa’s steam room, sauna and plunge pools can be accessed as part of the all-inclusive package. Spa and salon treatments are available at an extra cost.

Weddings at Hyatt Ziva Rose Hall

The spectacular scenery of Montego Bay is the ideal setting for an unforgettable wedding. The Hyatt Ziva boasts a number of ceremony and reception venues, including an oceanfront gazebo, grand ballroom and the beach itself. The resort’s dedicated team of wedding specialists help loved-up couples choose from a variety of comprehensive packages for a hassle-free “I do”. Honeymoon, vow-renewal and anniversary packages are also available.
