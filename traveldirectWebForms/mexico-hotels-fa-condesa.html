Fiesta Americana Condesa

All-inclusive and all-ages, Fiesta Americana Condesa provides lively luxury on Mexico's Caribbean coast. The beach resort offers a wide array of activities tailored to children, teens and adults, for a sunshine-filled holiday that everyone will enjoy. Its modern facilities include ten international restaurants, four bars, two swimming pools, a spa and fitness centre.

Fiesta Americana Condesa is located within Cancun’s Zona Hotelera (Hotel Zone), 20-minutes away from the international airport. A number of top tourist attractions are within walking distance including ancient ruins, designer fashion malls and a marina.

Rooms at Fiesta Americana Condesa

Contemporary Mexican design, fine furnishings and state-of-the-art electronics combine to create Fiesta Americana’s five-star accommodation. Each of Fiesta Americana’s 476 rooms and 26 suites feature king-sized beds with deluxe sheets, LCD televisions with satellite channels, internet access, coffee makers and private balconies or patios with stunning ocean views.

Bar Services at Fiesta Americana Condesa

Fiesta Americana Condesa’s four distinct bars serve up an array of tasty libations to enjoy as part of the all-inclusive package. Take in the sunset while sipping a margarita at the beach bar, enjoy a frothy cappuccino in the lobby or doggy paddle your way to a cool one at the swim-up bar.

Dining at Fiesta Americana Condesa

Fiesta Americana Condesa spans the globe with is 10 international restaurants spread throughout the scenic resort.

  • Kalmia: A buffet of international and regional cuisine, served against a stunning sea backdrop.
  • Kambu: Situated by the pool in a Mayan-style palapa, this restaurant boasts an international menu but focuses on Asian cuisine at dinner time.
  • San Telmo: An Argentinian steakhouse serving Angus beef, lobster, international wines and more. Includes a surcharge.
  • El Mexicano: A contemporary Mexican menu is complemented by fine décor, featuring local artworks and traditional talavera pottery.
  • Pizzeria: Sitting in the shade of palm trees, this classic pizzeria uses an authentic wood-fire oven.
  • Cevicheria: A casual beach joint specialising in fresh ceviche, shrimp tacos, hot salsas and draft beer.
  • Sushi Corner: Over 15 sushi and nigiri dishes are made to order at this late-night lounge.
  • Trattoria: An Italian trattoria-style restaurant with a menu of pastas, salads, paninis and more.
  • The Grill: An American grill with breathtaking ocean views.
  • Cantina: Authentic Mexican cuisine is accompanied by Cancun craft-beers at this traditional cantina.
  • Deli & Gift Shop: A relaxed spot for a pastry and cup of coffee.

Mexican Hospitality

Mexico’s renowned beauty and warmth of spirit collide in this spectacular beachfront resort. Providing the opportunity for romance, relaxation and revelry, Fiesta Americana Condesa Cancun is the perfect destination to unwind.

Entertainment at Fiesta Americana Condesa

When the sun goes down, the hotel lounge transforms into a theatre, providing nightly entertainment. Guests can enjoy live music performances, magic shows and karaoke evenings or make the short trip to downtown Cancun, home to some country’s liveliest nightlife spots.

Activities at Fiesta Americana Condesa

An ever-changing schedule of activities keeps guests entertained and active. The friendly animation team organise Zumba classes, water aerobics sessions, bean bag toss games and much more. The hotel’s information area provides a schedule of the week’s activities so you can plan your itinerary ahead of time.

The kids club ensures that the resort’s youngest guests are kept occupied and entertained. The programme, designed by a specialist children’s entertainment company, offers supervised pool and beach games, competitions, arts and crafts and more. Little ones will be delighted by the kids’ pool area which features a pirate ship playground, complete with slides and water cannons. Teens are treated to video games, pool tables and film screenings.

Fiesta Americana Condesa Spa

Miluma is Fiesta Americana Condesa’s full-service spa. The luxury facility offers an array of indulgent treatments, incorporating traditional Mexican products and methods with the latest in beauty and wellness technology. Facilities include a steam room, sauna, spa tub, immersion pool, pressure showers, hydrotherapy, 11 outdoor treatment rooms and an open-air gym.

Weddings at Fiesta Americana Condesa

Fiesta Americana Condesa’s stunning surroundings, high-quality facilities and dedicated team of wedding professionals, makes it an ideal place to say “I do”. The resort offers a number of wedding packages that take care of all the details, big and small, from the bride’s bouquet to breakfast in bed. Couples can say their vows on the beach or in the resort’s intimate lounge.
