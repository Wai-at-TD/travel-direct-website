This boutique resort offers a luxury all-inclusive experience in a gorgeous beachfront setting. Every building of the resort has been designed to sit in harmony with the bay’s natural environment. The lobby, restaurant, bar, sanctuary and pool lie on a lush slope overlooking the Caribbean Sea, perfect for catching one of Saint Lucia’s renowned sunsets.

Calabash Cove sits on a palm tree-lined beach in the north west of the island. Though the resort feels secluded, Rodney Bay Marina is just 3.7 miles away and the capital city of Castries can be reached in under 15 minutes.

Accommodation

Calabash Cove: Luxury, Premium All-inclusive Resort

Tranquil and welcoming, Calabash Cove is quintessentially Caribbean. The resort’s stunning suites, fabulous restaurant and world-class spa combine for a luxury experience.

Calabash Cove lies nestled amongst the palm trees and white beaches of Saint Lucia’s Caribbean Coast. Hewanorra International Airport is less than 40 miles away.

Rooms

All accommodation features a private balcony or patio with picturesque views of the surrounding area. Deluxe amenities help to ensure a comfortable stay.

This package includes the Sunset Ocean View Junior Suite. If you would like an upgrade to any of the different rooms or suites offered by the resort, please get in touch with your Travel Direct holiday specialist.

Activities

Guests can spend their days enjoying the infinity pool, modern fitness centre or stretch of private beach. Calabash Cove also provides a dedicated chill-out room called Whispers. Those keen to see more of Saint Lucia should contact the leisure concierge service.
