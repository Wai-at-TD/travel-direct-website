Virgin Atlantic

Cuba Direct is delighted to be working in partnership with Virgin Atlantic. For over 30 years, it’s been one of the world’s best loved longhaul airlines, annually flying over six million people to top global destinations.

So step onboard and you’ll experience the unique spirit, intuitive service and customer-focused innovation that make it an airline people can’t wait to fly.

Economy Cabin

  • Start your holiday by easily managing your booking at virginatlantic.com. Choose your seat in advance* via fast online or mobile check in.
  • Relax onboard in your comfy seat with 31in (over 78cm) seat pitch. Or buy even more space with an Extra Legroom or Exit Row seat.
  • Choose from three tasty meals, plus complimentary drinks and snacks throughout.
  • Settle in with the latest and greatest award winning entertainment.
  • Stay connected with onboard WiFi**.
  • Know the kids are looked after with special movies, meals and activity books.
  • On night flights, freshen up with your Relax Pack, sip a soothing hot chocolate and take a snooze.
  • Enjoy our intuitive, personal service throughout.
More to make your journey fly

Start your holiday by easily managing your booking at virginatlantic.com. Check in quickly online or on your mobile and choose your seat in advance* if you like. So at the airport, you can just drop your bags off and enjoy some free time.

Onboard, your comfortable seat has room to relax or sleep, with an adjustable headrest and 31in (over 78cm) seat pitch. And if you bought an Extra legroom or Exit Row seat, you’ll be stretching out in even more space.

For dinner, you can choose from three tasty main meals, including a vegetarian option. With complimentary drinks and snacks available throughout the flight.

When you’ve settled in, it’s time to meet Vera, our award winning inflight entertainment system. Vera offers a fantastic choice of over 300 hours of the latest and greatest movies, TV shows and music to enjoy with your movie snack**. And you can stay connected with onboard WiFi***.

On night flights, we hand you a complimentary Relax Pack. It’s packed with useful things, including a toothbrush and toothpaste, socks, eye mask and earplugs. So you can sip your soothing hot chocolate then get ready to snooze.

If you’re travelling with kids from the UK, we hand them a pack full of stuff to help them enjoy a great flight. There’s also dedicated kids’ movie and TV and you can order kids’ meals before you fly.

Throughout the flight, our friendly crew offer you personal, intuitive service that sets you up for a great holiday.

* You can choose your seat for free within 24 hours of departure, or from £25 (£50 per round trip) in advance of this.
** Available on selected flights for a fee.

Premium Economy

  • Speed through the airport thanks to special priority check in and boarding.
  • Relax preflight with a glass of welcome bubbly and newspaper in our dedicated cabin.
  • Recline on your wide leather seat with headrest, footrest and 38in (over 96cm) seat pitch.
  • Enjoy your choice of three delicious main dishes, upgraded with china crockery, cutlery and linen.
  • Finish your meal with an after dinner liqueur.
  • Snooze or freshen up on night flights with your Relax Pack.
  • Stay connected with inseat power and onboard WiFi*.
  • Help yourself to tempting snacks and soft drinks whenever you like.
  
Your time, your space

The smoothest of journeys begins when you arrive at the airport, with special priority check in and boarding.

Onboard in the dedicated Premium Economy cabin, we offer you a preflight welcome drink to toast your journey, before you settle in with your choice of newspaper.

There’s more space to relax, because your leather seat is one of the biggest in its class. It’s up to 21in (over 53cm) wide, with a footrest, headrest and 38in (over 96cm) seat pitch. Inseat power means you can keep your gadgets going during the flight. And onboard WiFi* helps you stay connected.

At dinner time, you can choose from three delicious main meals, upgraded with china crockery, cutlery and linen. To finish your meal perfectly, we offer you a liqueur.

Then there’s Vera. Our inflight entertainment system is the perfect way to unwind, with over 300 hours of the latest and greatest award winning entertainment to choose from.

On night flights, we also hand you a useful Relax Pack to help you drift into sleep. It includes a toothbrush and toothpaste, eye mask and earplugs.

Depending on the time of day, we offer you a hot breakfast or light meal to enjoy before we land, setting you up for the next part of your journey.

On arrival, we make sure you and your priority tagged bags are among the first off the aircraft, for easy onward holiday travel.

* Available on selected flights for a fee.

Upper Class

  • Enjoy the effortless comfort of complimentary chauffeur driven car* transfers to and from the airport.
  • At London Heathrow, breeze through the Private Security Channel within our Upper Class Wing.
  • Take time out in our incredible Clubhouse** and choose from our varied food and fine wine menu.
  • Onboard, relax or socialise at the Upper Class bar, it’s your choice.
  • Stretch out in your super comfortable Upper Class suite.
  • Stay connected with inseat laptop power and onboard WiFi***.
  • Finish off your imaginatively designed meal with cheese and port.
  • Enjoy a great night’s sleep in your spacious, fully flat bed.
  • On landing in the morning at London Heathrow, get ready to take the day on at our welcoming Revivals† lounge.
More to make your journey fly

Effortless travel, your way

Ease into your experience with a complimentary chauffeur driven car* transfer to the airport. Departing from London Heathrow, you glide right up to the Upper Class Wing, where the team greet you and swiftly handle your luggage. Then breeze through our Private Security Channel to the Clubhouse, where you’ll be relaxing within ten minutes.

Beautifully designed and welcoming, our Clubhouse is an incredible space to unwind. Sip a cocktail as you take your pick from the creative food and drink menu. At London and New York JFK, you can also enjoy a complimentary treatment or haircut** at the Clubhouse Spa.

Onboard, your Upper Class Suite awaits. Its unique design gives you direct aisle access with the space to relax in privacy and comfort. Inseat power and onboard WiFi mean you can stay connected***.

The Upper Class menu offers an imaginatively designed range of elegant hot and cold dishes, desserts and fine wines to choose from. Cheese and port finish your meal perfectly.

After dinner, you can stretch your legs at the onboard bar. It’s your space to socialise or just relax with a drink. Whatever you feel like.

When you’re ready, your suite easily transforms into a spacious fully flat bed. Change into your Upper Class sleep suit, settle under the soft duvet and enjoy refreshing, undisturbed sleep.

Before we land, we serve you a hot breakfast or light meal, depending on the time of day.

If you’d rather sleep in and your flight arrives into London Heathrow in the morning, you can head to the Revivals† lounge. Where a delicious cooked breakfast and hot shower help you take the day on.

* Chauffeur driven car service available at most destinations with selected Upper Class fares (J, D, I & C class). Mileage restrictions apply.
** Lounge facilities vary at each destination.
*** Available on selected flights for a fee.
† Open daily 5.00am-1.30pm.
