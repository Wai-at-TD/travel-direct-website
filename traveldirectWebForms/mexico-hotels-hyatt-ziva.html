Hyatt Ziva

Surrounded by azure waters, white sand and swaying palm trees, Hyatt Ziva is an all-inclusive paradise for the whole family. Adults can relax in a beach cabana, explore the microbrewery or unwind at the oceanfront spa while children will enjoy the Kidz Club, water play area and games room. The resort features nine restaurants, seven bars and three infinity pools.

The Hyatt Ziva sits adjacent to the bustle of Cancun’s entertainment district. Set on a peninsula, it is the only resort in the region to be surrounded on three sides by the Caribbean Sea.

Rooms at Hyatt Ziva

Each of Hyatt Ziva’s 547 suites feature luxury furnishings, floor-to-ceiling windows, balconies or terraces and spa-style bathrooms.

  • Standard: Available with king or double bed. Garden views from a walk-out terrace. 483 square feet.
  • Resort View: Available with king or double bed. Private balcony overlooks the resort. 483 square feet.
  • Ocean View: Available with king or double bed. Features views of the Caribbean Sea. 483 square feet.
  • Ocean Front: Available with king or double bed. Private balcony boasts sweeping views of the Caribbean Sea. 483 square feet.
  • Ocean Front Master: Situated in an adults-only tower. Available with king or double bed. Private balcony boasts sweeping views of the Caribbean Sea. Guests are granted access to restaurants at the nearby Hyatt Zilara for dinner. 695 square feet.
  • Dolphin View Master: Available with king or double bed. Overlooks the neighbouring dolphin habitat. Bathroom includes a jetted bathtub.
  • Sky Ocean Front Master Suite: Situated in an adults-only tower. Available with king or double bed. The private balcony features a hot-tub and sea views. Guests are granted access to restaurants at the nearby Hyatt Zilara for dinner. 695 square feet.
Swim-up rooms

Swim-up rooms grant guests direct access to a semi-private pool.

  • Swim-Up: Available with king or double bed. Enjoys sea views. Only available for those aged 13 and over. 712 square feet.
  • Sky Swim Up Ocean Front Master: Situated in an adults-only tower and includes access to Hyatt Zilara restaurants for dinner. Enjoys sweeping sea views. 695 square feet.
Club rooms

Guests staying in Club rooms enjoy a range of additional benefits and services, including access to the Hyatt Ziva Club lounge and private check-in.

  • Club Ocean Front: Available with king or double bed. The private balcony overlooks the Caribbean Sea. 463 square feet.
  • Club Ocean Front Corner Suite: Bedroom has a king-sized bed. The private balcony features a hot-tub and sea views. 1,087 square feet.
  • Club Ocean Front Master Suite: Bedroom has a king-sized bed. The private balcony features a hot-tub and sea views. 1,524 square feet.
  • Club Two Bedroom Ocean Front Suite: Encompasses two bedrooms, one with a king-sized bed and one with two double beds The private balcony features a hot-tub and sea views. 2,115 square feet.
Luxurious Suites

These luxurious suites are the resort’s most spacious accommodation.

  • Presidential Suite: Bedroom has a king-sized bed. Spread over two floors. Features two balconies with Caribbean Sea views. 1,316 square feet.
  • Pyramid Suite: Spread over the entire 18th floor. Private balcony overlooks the Caribbean Sea. Guests enjoy Club benefits.

Bar Services at Hyatt Ziva

The expansive resort is home to seven different bars, each with its own vibe and specialities.

  • Saasil Bar: Lobby bar with a chic interior and scenic terrace.
  • Juana Margarita?: Boasts a comprehensive menu of local tequilas.
  • Tres Cerveza’s: A sports bar with owns microbrewery and brew master.
  • Punta Vista: Set by the adults-only rooftop pool, this a casual spot with spectacular views.
  • Dips & Sips: A swim-up bar with a superb cocktail menu.
  • 24 Horas: A lively lounge serving drinks and snacks 24 hours a day.
  • Bar del Mar: This pool bar is reserved exclusively for guests staying in Hyatt Club rooms.

Dining at Hyatt Ziva

Hyatt Ziva features nine atmospheric restaurants, serving up dishes from around the world.

  • El Mercado: A gourmet buffet of exciting international dishes. Open for breakfast, lunch and dinner.
  • The Moongate: A Pan-Asian evening spot, featuring dim sum, stir fry, Teppanyaki and more.
  • La Bastille: An elegant adults-only dinner restaurant serving classic French favourites.
  • Tradewinds: A casual grill by day and steakhouse by night.
  • Lorenzo’s: A trattoria-style restaurant with a wood-fire pizza oven. Open for lunch and dinner.
  • Chevy’s: An American diner with a classic menu of burgers, hot dogs and shakes. Open for lunch and dinner.
  • Pasteles: A dessert parlour with sinfully sweet treats.
  • Habaneros: Latin American lunch and dinner dishes are served directly on the beach.
  • Casa Café: A coffee house where expert baristas make espressos, cappuccinos and more. Open for breakfast, lunch and dinner, guests can enjoy sandwiches, snacks and pastries.

Entertainment at Hyatt Ziva

Guests can enjoy live entertainment every night, including traditional Mexican music and dance performances. Some Cancun’s liveliest entertainment spots sit just moments away from the hotel.

Activities at Hyatt Ziva

The resort boasts a wide range of state-of-the-art recreation facilities, including a lighted rooftop tennis court, fitness centre and three swimming pools. The picturesque beach is home to a volleyball court, non-motorised water sports equipment and, for those who prefer to take things easy, comfortable sunbeds.

Kids will enjoy the varied activities of the Kidz Club while teens will find plenty to do in the game room.

Excursions at Hyatt Ziva

A dedicated excursions desk is available to help guests arrange trips and tours around Mexico, including scuba diving, tequila tasting and zip lining. A dolphin habitat sits directly next to the resort, offering interactive dolphin experiences for an additional fee.

Hyatt Ziva Spa

Eco holistic, Mayan inspired and totally relaxing, the onsite spa is the perfect place to rejuvenate the mind, body and soul. Massage treatments and body rituals take place in one of the tranquil treatment rooms or in a beach palapa. The spa also features a whirlpool tub, steam room, sauna, cold plunge pool and beauty salon.

Weddings at Hyatt Ziva

Loved-up couples can say their vows in one of the Caribbean’s most romantic locations with Hyatt Ziva. The resort offers a number of comprehensive wedding packages, including a complimentary service, for a stress-free “I do” experience. The hotel’s dedicated wedding centre will help you plan your perfect big day.
