Butterfly Beach Hotel

Offering spacious well-equipped rooms in a superb location, Butterfly Beach Hotel is remarkably good value. The laidback resort boasts access to two peaceful white-sand beaches yet restaurants, bars, shops and tourist attractions are all within easy reach. The lush grounds encompass a full service restaurant, swimming pool and gift shop.

Butterfly Beach Hotels sits in the centre of Barbados’ lively south coast. Guests are in the heart of island action with Oistins in walking distance and the renowned nightlife and restaurant area of St Lawrence’s Gap just a five-minutes drive away. The capital city of Bridgetown can be reached in under twenty minutes.

Rooms at Butterfly Beach Hotel

Butterfly Beach encompasses 93 rooms, studios and apartments set across three different buildings. All accommodation comes equipped with air-conditioning, cable television, wired internet access and a ceiling fan. With several self-catering categories available, guests can prepare meals and snacks in their rooms to enjoy on their private balcony or terrace.

Room types

  • Room Mixed View: Overlooks the whirlpool courtyard towards Oistins Bay. A small fridge and kettle are provided.
  • Studio Island View: Enjoys scenic island views. A kitchenette is provided with a fridge, sink, toaster, kettle and hobs.
  • Superior Studio Island View: Offers upgraded furnishings such as bedside USB charging ports, a flat screen television and microwave.
  • Superior Studio Ocean View: Is equipped with the same upgraded furnishings as the Superior Island View Studio but also features stunning views of the Caribbean Sea.
  • One Bedroom Apartment Island View: A home away from home with a full-kitchen and separate bedroom.

Bar and Dining Services at Butterfly Beach Hotel

Overlooking the sparkling waters of the Caribbean Sea, the onsite Reef Bar and Grill is a scenic spot for breakfast, lunch, dinner and snacks or to simply kick back with a rum cocktail. Guests can enjoy a wide range of Caribbean and European dishes in a casual atmosphere, expertly prepared by the resort’s chefs. The lounge café area also offers a full drinks service.

Caribbean Hospitality

Barbados’ renowned beauty, cuisine and warmth of spirit collide in this spectacular coastal resort. Providing the opportunity for romance, relaxation and revelry, Butterfly Beach Hotel Barbados is the perfect destination to unwind.

Entertainment at Butterfly Beach Hotel

Special entertainment evenings take place three nights a week, including BBQ buffet nights, karaoke show downs and a rum punch party. These spirited evenings are the perfect opportunity to experience the local music scene with live steel pan and guitar performances occurring regularly. The lounge area boasts a large-screen TV where guests can watch sport matches.

AN ARRAY OF ACTIVITIES TO ENJOY

Many guests choose to spend their days splashing and swimming in the resort’s large freshwater swimming pool. It’s surrounded by an ample sundeck furnished with loungers for those who want to get stuck into a good holiday read or work on their tan. The resort also enjoys access to two stunning Caribbean beaches.

Excursions at Butterfly Beach Hotel

The onsite activities desk is dedicated to helping guests experience the best of Barbados. They can help organise a range of tours as well as car rentals for those who want to discover the island at their own pace. Popular excursions include snorkelling adventures and a trip to Oistin’s Fish Fry.
