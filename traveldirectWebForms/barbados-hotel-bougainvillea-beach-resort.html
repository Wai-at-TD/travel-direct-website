Bougainvillea Beach Resort Resort & Spa

Kick back, relax and set your watch to island time at Bougainvillea Beach Resort . This luxury hotel offers superb facilities and beautifully-furnished rooms, set against the splendour of a white sand beach and tropical gardens. Located just a stone’s-throw away from some of the country’s top tourist attractions, Bougainvillea is perfectly suited to cultural holidays and relaxation escapes alike.
Watch the video and see for yourself. Bougainvillea is an oasis of calm and relaxation, where guests can laze on the beach, feast on fine food and indulge in reviving spa treatments. Those that can tear themselves away from the soft sand have access to fantastic sports facilities.

The property sits on Barbados’ lively South Coast, just twenty minutes from the capital city, Bridgetown.

Rooms at Bougainvillea Beach Resort

Suites are spacious and fully furnished with modern conveniences. All accommodation includes a kitchen or kitchenette, sitting area and balcony or patio.

This package includes the Superior Studio. If you would like an upgrade to any of the different rooms or suites offered by the resort, please get in touch with your Travel Direct holiday specialist.

Room types

  • Superior Studio: Equipped with a kitchenette and L-shaped sofa. The bathroom features a walk-in shower. Approximately 376 square feet / 35 square meters.
  • Junior Suite: Equipped with a kitchenette and separate seating area. The bathroom features a combination bathtub-shower. Approximately 438 square feet / 40 square meters.
  • Honeymoon Suite: Equipped with a kitchenette and separate seating area with sofa bed. The bathroom features a combination bathtub-shower and the king-sized bed is four poster. Approximately 323 square feet / 30 square meters.
  • One Bedroom Suite: Equipped with a kitchen and living room with sofa bed. The bathroom features a shower. Approximately 476 square feet / 44.2 square meters.
  • Deluxe One Bedroom Suite: Equipped with a kitchen and living room with sofa bed. The bathroom features a double vanity and combination bathroom-shower. Approximately 585 – 603 square feet / 54.3 - 56 square meters.
  • One Bedroom Penthouse: Equipped with a kitchen and living room with sofa bed. The bathroom features a combination bathroom-shower. Approximately 816 – 1406 square feet / 75.5 - 130.6 square meters.
  • Deluxe Two Bedroom Suite: Equipped with a kitchen and living room with sofa bed. The bathroom features a combination bathtub-shower. One Queen bedroom and one King. Approximately 775 square feet / 70 square meters.
  • Two Bedroom Beachfront: Equipped with a kitchen and living room with sofa bed. There are two bathrooms with combination bathtub-showers. One master King bedroom and one Queen. Approximately 691-1100 square feet / 64.2 – 102.3 square meters.

Bar Services at Bougainvillea Beach Resort

Bougainvillea’s pool bar serves up delicious drinks made by expert bartenders. Choose from an enticing menu of cocktails, beers, wines and soft drinks and simply swim-up when you’re ready to order. The local Banks Beer and Bajan Rum Punch are particularly popular. The hotel restaurants are romantic spots for sunset cocktails.

Dining at Bougainvillea Beach Resort

Guests can enjoy fine cuisine in gorgeous settings at Bougainvillea’s restaurants.

  • Lanterns by the Sea Restaurant: The hotel’s signature restaurant, Lanterns by the Sea is open for breakfast, lunch and dinner. Guests can feast on Bajan and international cuisine while enjoying superb South Coast views.
  • Water’s Edge: By day, this tranquil eatery serves up traditional afternoon teas, pastries and cakes. In the evening, it transforms into a romantic beachfront restaurant. The “Small Plates, Big Flavours” concept allows guests to sample several options off the menu, ideal for those who just can’t choose.

Caribbean Hospitality

Barbados’ renowned beauty, cuisine and warmth of spirit collide in this spectacular beachfront resort. Providing the opportunity for romance, relaxation and revelry, Bougainvillea is the perfect destination to unwind.

AN ARRAY OF ACTIVITIES TO ENJOY

Thrill-seekers, sun-worshipers and culture vultures alike can find plenty of ways to spend their holiday at Bougainvillea Beach Resort. The grounds include a tennis courts, a spa, a games room, beach volleyball courts, three swimming pools and much more. A water sports hut sits on the private beach, providing guests with non-motorised water sports equipment.

Excursions at Bougainvillea Beach Resort

The Bougainvillea activities team help guests to experience the best Barbados has to offer, with advice on tourist attractions to visit, bars to dance at and festivals you can’t miss. The friendly staff also help guests to arrange cultural and adventure excursions. Book in advance.

Bougainvillea Beach Resort Spa

Waves Beauty Spa & Salon offers a premium spa and salon services in a truly tranquil setting. Treatments include manicures, pedicures, waxing, facials, massages, scrubs and more.

Weddings at Bougainvillea Beach Resort

It doesn’t get much more romantic than saying “I do” on a Caribbean beach. Couples can choose from two stunning locations, a private ocean-front garden (for parties up to 70) or with their feet in the sand (for parties up to 12). The resort offers a number of comprehensive wedding packages for stress-free nuptials.
