Grand Velas

Designed to reflect the natural splendour of Riviera Maya, Grand Velas is chic, contemporary and authentically Mexican. The all-inclusive resort encompasses 491 luxury suites, six bars, eight restaurants, an infinity pool, an award-winning spa and a wide range of family-friendly facilities.

Nestled on the white beaches of Riviera Maya, Grand Velas bordered by blue waters of the Caribbean Sea and lush Yucatan jungle. The fashionable downtown area of Playa del Carmen is just five-minutes away.

Rooms, Services, and Facilities. Decidedly Accommodating

Grand Velas

Rooms at Grand Velas

Guests can choose from three different category of suite; each category has its own ambiance but all feature plush furnishings inspired by the natural landscape. Plunge pools, whirlpool tubs, 42” plasma TVs and wireless internet are just some of the luxury amenities provided.

  • Grand Class: Designed with relaxation in mind, Grand Class suites are reserved exclusively for adults and teenagers. The 1,300 sq.-ft. suites include Mexican artwork, lounge areas and hot tubs. Terraces feature plunge pools and overlook the Caribbean Sea.
  • Ambassador: Family-friendly Ambassador suites span 1,200 sq.ft to include spacious sitting areas and bathrooms with Jacuzzis. Terraces overlook the sea and the Kid’s Club is nearby.
  • Zen Jungle: Mayan Jungle views complement the exotic furnishings of these popular all-ages suites. The accommodation features 1,100 sq. ft. of living space, hot tubs and private terrace.

Bar Services at Grand Velas

Grand Velas boasts six fashionable bars, serving up coffees, juices, cocktails and more.

  • Sky Bar: A hip rooftop bar with superb sunset views.
  • Sen Lin Bar: A chic lounge specialising in exotic libations.
  • Aqua Bar: A pool bar with both swim-up and dry seating.
  • Karaoke Bar: A lively scene where guests belt out their favourite tunes.
  • Piano Bar: A classy spot to mingle with live music and a premium drinks menu.
  • Koi Bar: A luxury sports bar, this is the perfect place to catch the big game.

Dining at Grand Velas

Grand Velas is home to some of the finest restaurants in Riviera Maya. Acclaimed chefs from around the world delight guests with expertly-prepared international cuisine. Whether enjoying a candlelit dinner or poolside snacks, guests can expect top quality ingredients and delicious flavours.

  • Piaf: Inspired by 1940s Paris, this is a refined French dinner restaurant open to adults only.
  • Frida: Named after legendary Mexican artist Frida Kahlo, the menu of this gourmet restaurant reflects Mexico’s rich culinary heritage.
  • Azul: A casual all-day restaurant with a popular open-air terrace. The menu features dishes from around the world, including hamburgers, sushi, tacos and pizza.
  • Bistro: Overlooking the ocean, this French-style bistro is one of the resort’s most romantic spots. Open all day but to adults-only.
  • Chaká: Nestled amongst the mangroves, this is a casual open-air restaurant with a fusion of international cuisines.
  • Sen Lin: A lively restaurant with dishes from across Asia.
  • Cocina de Autor: A sophisticated adults-only spot specialising in Mexican and Caribbean food.
  • Luca: This Italian-Mediterranean restaurant boasts a mouth-watering menu of fresh seafood, meats and homemade pasta.

Mexican Hospitality

Mexico’s renowned beauty and warmth of spirit collide in this spectacular beachfront resort. Providing the opportunity for romance, relaxation and revelry, Grand Velas is the perfect destination to unwind.

Entertainment at Grand Velas

Evenings are lively, with regular musical performance and dancing. The Karaoke Bar is a great spot to both entertain and be entertained. Teenagers can attend a nightly supervised disco, complete with a bar serving soft drinks and snacks.

Activities at Grand Velas

There are plenty of ways to enjoy Grand Velas’ beautiful beach location, with complimentary kayaks, pedal boats and snorkelling sets all on offer. Those keen to stay active on holiday can make use of the onsite gym, infinity pool, daily sports programme and fitness classes.

Younger guests are well catered for with a Kids Club and Teen Club. Children aged 5 to 12 can enjoy storytelling, films, drawing, painting and board games in a safe environment, supervised by a highly-qualified professional. Teenagers have access to billiard table, arcade games, X-box, PS3, 40” LED TV and more.

Excursions at Grand Velas

Guests can discover more about the rich culture and environment of Riviera Maya through an array of excursions. Grand Velas’ travel agency offers a wide variety trips and experiences, including jungle tours, dolphin swimming experiences and scuba diving trips.

Grand Velas Spa

Named “Best Spa in the World” by Virtuoso in 2010, this wellness sanctuary delivers top quality treatments in a calming jungle-inspired environment. Guests can choose from massages, wraps, facials and manicures as well as more specialist therapies. The spa’s signature Riviera Maya Water Journey features a clay room, ice room and experience pool.

Weddings at Grand Velas

The immense beauty of Mexico’s Caribbean coast provides the perfect setting for a truly romantic big day. Grand Velas employs a dedicated team of wedding experts to help couples tie the knot. With intimate garden parties, beach blow-outs and lavish banquets all on offer, the dream “I do” is within reach.
