Jewel Dunn's River

Experience Jamaica’s luxury, all-inclusive, adults-only oceanfront Jewel Dunn’s River Beach Resort & Spa, located just moments from the spectacular Dunn’s River Falls, one of Jamaica’s must-see attractions. The Jewel Dunn’s River Beach Resort & Spa boasts 250 beautifully appointed guest rooms and suites within three buildings – Diamond, Sapphire and Emerald. Rooms at Jewel Dunn's River

The Jewel Dunn's River has a total of 250 guest rooms within three buildings: Diamond, Sapphire and Emerald Lanai. Fresh white walls, luxurious linens and plush towels complement the dark patina of the mahogany furniture.

  • Premier Guest Room: Generously-sized standard guest rooms with limited views. All rooms have a dramatic 4-poster king bed with elegant, some with private balcony.
  • Emerald Lanai Guest Room: Located in a tropical garden setting, the ground floor rooms all open to their own outdoor furnished patio.
  • Sapphire Mountain View Guest Room: Mountain View guest rooms in the Sapphire Tower offer picturesque views of the lush peaks surrounding Ocho Rios.

More room types

  • Sapphire Ocean View Guest Room: All rooms have a private balcony, a welcoming 4-poster king bed with elegant Colonial decor and refreshing tiled floors.
  • Diamond Ocean View Concierge: spacious rooms offer views of the sparkling Caribbean Sea from a completely private balcony.
  • 1-bedroom Ocean View Butler Suite: With stunning ocean views from oversized balconies, the Ocean View Butler Suite also includes room service, plush his and hers robes with slippers and a personal butler.
  • Honeymoon Haven 1-Bedroom Ocean Front Suite: The 1-bedroom oceanfront Honeymoon Haven Butler suites lavish guests with over-the-top pampering and dramatic direct ocean vistas. The bedroom features a magnificent 4-poster canopied king bed with elegant mahogany accents. Refreshing tile covers the floor. The oversized bath has dual sinks, an enormous whirlpool and separate shower. Walk-in closets make it easy to store belongings and hang clothes.

Diving at Jewel Dunn’s River Resort

Whether a beginner, amateur or professional diver, discover a whole new world underwater while exploring oceanic treasures of all kinds. From beautiful marine life to never-before-seen fish, guests of Jewel Dunn’s River Resort are in for an adventure they’ll remember long after their trip is over. The vibrant waters of the Caribbean are some of the most desirable diving sites in the world. Warm and often clear, the water has higher levels of visibility than many dive sites. Unique fish, colorful reefs and rare creatures will most certainly captivate divers of all levels.

While diving is not part of the all-inclusive package at Jewel Dunn’s River Resort, we encourage visitors to safely explore and immerse themselves in the wonders of a scuba vacation — be it for one day or an entire stay. Our resort offers a number of scuba diving options — for additional fees — that will happily take visitors under the sea.

If you’re a first-time or non-certified diver: We suggest taking the Discover Scuba course that includes one amazing dive after certain requirements are met.
If you’re an amateur diver looking to become certified: Open water certifications can be obtained in as little as three days.
If you’re already a certified diver: Explore the spectacular sites around Jewel Runaway Bay with some of our experienced dive instructors.

Payment for scuba lessons, dives and certification courses are done via room charge.
