Fairmont Mayakoba

Nestled amongst 240 acres of tropical forest, the Fairmont Mayakoba is a luxury resort where the environment reigns supreme. Vehicles are abandoned at the entrance of the property to maintain the resort's delicate ecology and all transport within is accomplished on lanchas (covered boats), electric golf carts, bicycles or on foot. The shoreline is just a short stroll away for most, though some exclusive hotel suites sit adjacent to the white-sand beach. The lush greenery and sparkling waterways are accompanied by five swimming pools, three bars, four eateries and a state-of-the-art spa.

The Fairmont Mayakoba sits in the centre of Playa del Carmen, an exclusive gated community within the “Venice of the Caribbean”, Riviera Maya. Bustling Cancun is a 50-minute drive away and the region's international airport can be reached in 35-minutes.

Rooms at Fairmont Mayakoba

Fairmont Mayakoba boasts 401 luxurious guest rooms. Most lie amongst the verdant forest that dominates the resort, set back from the sea, but some suites enjoy exclusive beachfront locations. All rooms feature a LCD television, CD player, radio, high-speed internet access, and air conditioning. Bedrooms adjoin to oversized spa-style bathrooms and furnished balconies or terraces.

  • Fairmont Room: 436-480 square feet with views of the tropical jungle.
  • Fairmont View Room: 436-480 square feet with views of the bamboo pool and lancha docking point.
  • Deluxe Casita: 458-504 square feet with views of the mangrove or tropical forest.
  • Signature Casita: 458-504 square feet with views of the lagoon and water canals.
Set in the private beach area, these suites offer an exclusive butler service:

  • Oceanfront Casita: 587 square feet with views of the Caribbean Sea.
  • Signature Casita Suite: 915-1,033 square feet with views of the lagoon and water canals. Features high ceilings, a wraparound balcony, separate living rooms and two bathrooms.
  • Beach Area Casita Suite: 1,113 square feet with views of the mangrove and lagoon from the bedroom and Caribbean Sea from the terrace. Features a separate living room, two bathrooms, a wraparound balcony and private rooftop pool.
  • Oceanfront Casita Suite: 963 square feet with views of the Caribbean Sea. Features high ceilings, separate living room, direct access to the beach from a private stairway and a private infinity pool.
  • Oceanfront Premium Suite: 1,075 square feet with views of the Caribbean Sea. Features a separate living and dining room, two bathrooms, a private stairway leading to the beach and a private infinity pool on the terrace.

Bar Services at Fairmont Mayakoba

The Fairmont Mayakoba resort encompasses three popular bars. Menus include Mexican craft-beers, Latin wines, Caribbean cocktails and a mammoth collection of tequila.

  • El Lobby Lounge: Tropical cocktails are served with tasty treats, including tapas, sharing platters, sushi and Mexican appetizers. The covered terrace overlooks the resort’s waterways and daily live music keeps the mood energetic.
  • Las Olas: A refreshing pool bar designed to reflect the region’s water canals.
  • Aqua: A swim-up pool bar serving juices, cocktails, beers and more.

Dining at Fairmont Mayakoba

Fairmont Mayakoba is home to four restaurants, recently redesigned with the help of world-renowned chef and restauranteur Richard Sandoval. The hotel’s commitment to the environment extends to its dining outlets with local, organic and sustainable ingredients being used wherever possible. In-room dining is available 24-hours a day.

  • Las Brisas: International fare featuring a local twist, enjoyed on a deck overlooking the Caribbean Sea. A raw bar offers a range of ceviches, fresh from ocean to table.
  • El Puerto: The resort’s signature restaurant, chefs serve up a varied menu including a number of innovative Latin-Asian fusion dishes. The beverage menu features over 100 tequilas along with Latin wines, Japanese sakes and more. These gourmet delights are complemented sweeping views of the picturesque property, including the lancha central docking point.
  • La Laguna: A relaxed all-day eatery with live music and Mexican-inspired craft cocktails. Breakfast is buffet-style while lunch and dinner are a la carte. Guests can dine inside or outside; the terrace overlooks the mangrove and water canals.
  • Café Maya: A relaxed café offering pastries, deli dishes, coffee and ice cream with traditional Mayan flavours. A number of culinary souvenirs are on sale, including Mexican seasonings.

Activities at Fairmont Mayakoba

This large resort buzzes with activity as guests get stuck in to a variety of onsite entertainment. There are five pools to enjoy, including a 10,000 square foot free-form pool, as well as a pristine Riviera beach supplied with water sport equipment. Guests enjoy complimentary access to tennis and paddle courts, with transportation to and from the courts included. Nature trails are dotted throughout the resort and BMX bikes are available to all guests. Those who want to pick up a new skill on holiday can take a cooking class or join a Mexican craft beer tasting.

Guests aged 4 to 12 will find plenty to do with the hotel Kids Club and children’s swimming pool, equipped with waterslide. Little explorers should join the Discovery Club and Adventure Camp for lots of extra experiences and lessons, including trips into the jungle, nature classes and craft-making. Teenagers will be delighted by the Teen Room by PlayStation, kitted out with PS3 consoles and HD televisions.

Excursions at Fairmont Mayakoba

Situated amongst superb scenery, enchanting history and charming towns, there’s plenty to do away from the Fairmont Mayakoba too. Guests are encouraged to visit the nearby Mayan archaeological sites, ecological theme parks and markets. The Riviera Maya is home to the world’s second largest coral reef and a number of local tour operators offer scuba diving and snorkelling trips of the natural wonder. Golf fans will be delighted by the resort’s proximity to world-class golf facilities. A complimentary shuttle service is offered to and from El Camaleón Golf Course, host of the only official PGA tour event in Mexico, and the Jim McClean Golf School.

Maritur Travel Agency has an office at Fairmont Mayakoba, located off the main lobby. They can help book excursions and tours and organise car rentals.

Fairmont Mayakoba Spa

The 37,000 square foot Willow Stream Spa boasts 20 beautiful treatment rooms, including a couple’s treetop suite, two mineral pools and steam rooms. Many of the deluxe treatments offered use ancient Mayan methods and incorporate indigenous ingredients.

Weddings at Fairmont Mayakoba

On-site wedding planners are on hand to help guests achieve the big day of their dreams. Brides and grooms-to-be can choose from an assortment of romantic resort venues, including the grand ballroom, lush lawn or even the beach. Comprehensive wedding packages ensure hassle-free nuptial planning and can be tailored to couples' exact specifications.
