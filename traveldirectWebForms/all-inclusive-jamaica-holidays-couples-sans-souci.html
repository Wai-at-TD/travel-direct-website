Accommodation

Couples Sans Souci: Luxury, Premium All-Inclusive Resort

A five-star boutique resort that redefines the meaning of "premium all-inclusive" as guests dive into a paradise of water sports and activities including water skiing, scuba diving and golf, island excursions and catamaran cruising, gourmet dining and the finest top shelf liquor, all included at this Couples Resort

Couples Sans Souci sits on a secluded stretch of pure white sand, tucked amidst the coconut trees. This beachfront playground promises long uninhibited days spent with sand between your toes.

Rooms

From the Deluxe Garden Rooms set amidst tropical foliage to the stunning beachfront suites, each of the 234 rooms at Couples Sans Souci evokes a casual eclectic style with its fresh use of colour, bold fabrics and striking local artwork. Private verandahs and balconies are furnished with tables and chairts and suites boast hammocks for two, extending the living space to the outside.

This package includes the Deluxe Garden Room. If you would like an upgrade to any of the four different room types offered by this luxurious resort, please get in touch with your Travel Direct travel specialist.
