Travel Direct Tours

Our wide range of tours are designed for those who want to make the most of their time in a country. They cover the best cultural attractions, historical sites and natural wonders that the country has to offer, along with some off-the-beaten track experiences. We take care of all the arrangements, allowing you to enjoy a stress-free adventure.

Vietnam Tours

Learn about Havana’s rich offering of Art Deco architecture with the help of an expert guide. You’ll tour the city in a classic American car and wander through the pedestrian-only streets of its historic core. The tour includes the Bacardi Building, Hotel Nacional and Lopez Serrano Building.

Colombia Tours

Follow in the footsteps of Marlon Brando, Ernest Hemingway and Sammy Davis Jr. by enjoying an evening at the Tropicana Cabaret. Music, dancing and dazzling costumes combine to create this world-famous spectacle.

Costa Rica Tours

Explore the stunningly-beautiful and UNESCO World Heritage listed Viñales Valley. The visit includes a tour of a tobacco plantation, limestone caves and a cigar factory.

Peru Tours

Explore Havana by foot and car, visiting some of its most important landmarks. The tour includes the Capitol Building, colonial squares, Arts Museum, Museum of the Revolution, Bacardi Building, Revolution Square and Havana’s seafront drive (Malécon).

Mexico Tours

One of Cuba’s most legendary nights out, listen to the surviving members of Buena Vista Social Club perform live.

Jamaica Tours

Experience the history, culture and taste of this iconic Caribbean beverage through an informative guided tour.

Barbados Tours

Uncover Hemingway’s favourite haunts, cruising through Havana in a classic American car. The tour includes Floridita Bar (home of the author’s much-loved daiquiri), Bodeguita del Medio (where he enjoyed his favourite mojitos), Cojimar fishing village and the Hemingway Museum.

Ecuador Tours

Sail and swim Cuba’s azure waters with a catamaran trip and snorkelling adventure. You’ll enjoy an all-inclusive lunch on the picturesque Blanco Key.

Saint Lucia Tours

Tour the Vedado, Plaza de la Revolución and Miramar districts of the city in a classic American car, stopping to admire some of the city’s finest buildings. Learn about Baroque, Neo-Classical, Art Deco and more on the streets of Old Havana with your architecture-expert guide.

Other Place Tours

Wander the streets of Old Havana with an expert guide before visiting Morro Castle for the cannon ceremony. Complete the experience with a set-menu dinner at an atmospheric restaurant.

Other Place Tours

Tour the city in a classic American Car and walk through Old Havana with your knowledgeable guide. You’ll visit the Rum Museum and watch the hand-rolling of Cuban cigars at the famous Partagás Factory.

Other Place Tours

Cruise through Havana, stopping at some at some of its most famous locales. The tour includes the Capitol Building, the National Theatre, El Prado, Havana’s seafront drive (Malécon), Presidents Avenue, Revolution Square, Columbus Cemetery and John Lennon Park.
