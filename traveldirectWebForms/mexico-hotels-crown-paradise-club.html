Crown Paradise Club

An idyllic setting of sun, white sand and turquoise water combined with modern facilities, gastronomic delights and luxurious accommodation; Crown Paradise Club delivers the dream Caribbean holiday. The five-star resort caters to all ages through a varied offering of daily activities and nightly entertainment. Guests can feast at seven restaurants and six bars as part of their all-inclusive package.

Situated on one of the finest beaches in Cancun’s Hotel Zone, Crown Paradise Club is 15-minutes away from the International Airport and 30 minutes from the city centre. The Mayan ruins at El Rey Zona Arquelogica are just an eight-minute stroll away.

Rooms, Services, and Facilities. Decidedly Accommodating

Crown Paradise Club

Rooms at Crown Paradise Club

Crown Paradise Club encompasses 617 rooms and suites, each boasting its own private balcony or terrace with stunning ocean views. Accommodation is air-conditioned and equipped with modern conveniences for a comfortable stay. Furnishings include an LED television, radio alarm clock, mini fridge (stocked daily) and coffee maker. The full bathrooms feature a combination bath and shower.

  • Standard Room Ocean View: Available with one king-sized or two double beds.
  • Standard Plus Room Ocean View: Features a king-sized bed and bunk bed with trundle bed.
  • Family Paradise Suite: Features a king sized bed and bunk bed with trundle bed. The suite is spread across two levels and has two full bathrooms.
  • Crown Club Room Ocean View: Exclusively for adults with enhanced facilities. The room features a double Jacuzzi, separate shower, bath and WC, floral arrangements and a mini sound system. Guests are treated to gifts on arrival and a complimentary lagoon cruise.

Bar Services at Crown Paradise Club

Crown Paradise Club is home to six distinct bars, all offering fine beverages in a relaxed and welcoming setting.

  • Collage Bar: Located in the lobby area and overlooking the ocean, this is a family-friendly drinking spot with ping pong tables, pool, air hockey and mini kids’ cinema. Teens can enjoy a non-alcoholic beverage at their own exclusive bar as part of the Jetix Club.
  • Las Brisas Bar: This poolside joint specialises in frozen drinks; a refreshing treat on a hot day.
  • Bar Trafalgar’s: A nautical themed bar with a dance floor, table games and a spectacular view of the Caribbean Sea. Access is limited to guests aged 18 and over.
  • Bar Crown Club Lounge: Set on the eighth floor of the Crown Tower, this laidback lounge enjoys panoramic views of the resort and surrounding area.
  • Lobby Bar: Found in the heart of the property, this is a lively spot with live music in the afternoons and a number of signature drinks.
  • Club Caribe Bar: Best known for its cocktails and divine lagoon views, this adults-only bar is a popular place to catch the sunset. Access is limited to guests aged 18 and over.

Dining at Crown Paradise Club

Crown Paradise boasts five specialty restaurants, two buffet restaurants and three snack bars, providing a massive variety in cuisine and atmosphere. Guests can also enjoy 24-hour room service with a small service charge per order.

  • La Palapa: An international buffet located in the pool, offering everything from Italian to Indonesian.
  • Fujiyama: Sushi, tempura, rice and more is offered at this oriental buffet.
  • Sans Soucis: A fine dining restaurant offering sophisticated French fare.
  • La Palapa:Los Gallos: Live music accompanies an extensive tequila menu at this authentic Mexican restaurant.
  • Fisherman’s: A fresh seafood restaurant with a number of Caribbean-inspired dishes, including delicious coconut shrimps.
  • La Piaza: Italian delicacies are served with a beautiful ocean backdrop.
  • Wayne’s Boots: A Wild West themed eatery featuring prime cuts of meat and a mechanical bull.
  • Tutuch Snack: A grill and snack area, conveniently located right by the pool.
  • Midway Stop Snack: Open from 10 pm till 7 am, this snack shack provides sandwiches, nachos and quesadillas for night owls.

Entertainment at Crown Paradise Club

After admiring one of the Yucatan Peninsula’s famed sunsets, guests can enjoy the evening’s entertainment. The onsite theatre hosts live interactive shows every night, performed by the in-house activities team. After, guests are encouraged to don their dancing shoes and head to the Nitro Disco Club, where drinks are on the house until 2:00 am.

Activities at Crown Paradise Club

Providing an enriching and entertaining holiday experience, Crown Paradise Club plays host to a variety of activities to suit all ages. The all-inclusive resort encompasses a range of sports facilities -- including two floodlit tennis courts, a multi-purpose court and beach volleyball court -- and a large pool where water polo and aqua aerobics take place. Guests can join a scuba diving demonstration, compete in a ping pong tournament, learn how to boogie board and much more.

The Kids Paradise Programme offers daily activities for children aged 4 to 12 as well as night time fun, such as glow parties, karaoke nights and Mexican fiestas. The Jetix Club ensures teens are taken care of too, with friendly matches, video games, films and non-motorised water sports. The resorts tiniest visitors will enjoy Baby Paradise, a complimentary nursery with an indoor and outdoor play area.

Excursions at Crown Paradise Club

Rich in history, scenery and adventure the Yucatan Peninsula is a traveller's delight. Crown Paradise Club can arrange a number of tours throughout the region, covering some of Mexico's top tourist attractions.

  • Snorkelling and Scuba Diving
  • Xcaret
  • Tulum
  • Chichen Itza
  • Swimming with Dolphins

Crown Paradise Club Spa

The resort’s luxury spa offers a range of treatments and facilities for guests seeking the ultimate in relaxation. Facials, massages, scrubs, hydrotherapies and salon services are all on offer in a truly sumptuous setting.

Weddings at Crown Paradise Club

Couples dreaming of a wedding in paradise will be delighted by this luxury resort’s range of comprehensive wedding packages. The bride and groom can declare their everlasting love for one another with their toes in the sand of a picturesque beach before enjoying their romantic honeymoon at Crown Paradise Club.
