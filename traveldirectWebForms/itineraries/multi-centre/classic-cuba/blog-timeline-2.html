Days 3 to 5: Vinales

Transfer Havana to Vinales

Marvel at the ever-green Valley of Vinales as you wade through the brazen copper-hued tobacco farms, plantations and mountains. The transfer from your hotel in Havana lasts about 1 1/2 hours.

  • Tranfer choices:
  • Coach
  • Taxi

Explore The Valley of Vinales

Discover Cuba's most beautiful natural landscapes at the Valley of Vinales,UNESCO World Heritage Site since 1999. Learn to roll cigars on a visit to a local tobacco farm. Explore Caves, underground rivers, and mogotes

Transfer Vinales to Trinidad

This morning your transfer will pick you up and take you to Cuba's best preserved colonial town: Trinidad. It's a long journey but with lots of interesting stops along the way.
