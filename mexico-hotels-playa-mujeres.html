Finest Playa Mujeres

Part of the esteemed Excellence Group, Finest Playa Mujeres is the company’s first all-ages resort in Caribbean. Guests can expect the same high-quality facilities, glamorous design and spectacular scenery as the group’s other properties along with family-friendly extras. The all-inclusive hotel boasts an impressive array of dining, entertainment and activity facilities with 12 restaurants, 16 bars and lounges, 12 pools and a large luxury spa.

This brand new resort sits on a scenic white sand peninsula within the exclusive gated community of Playa Mujeres. The international airport is 25-minutes away and the bustle of downtown Cancun is even closer.

Rooms at Finest Playa Mujeres

At a minimum size of 800 square feet, Finest Playa Mujeres suites are super spacious and finely appointed. Each room features an array of luxurious furnishings including sumptuous beds, large sofas, wide-screen televisions and oversized Jacuzzis. Bathrooms are spa-style, with rain-showers for two and high-quality amenities. Every suite boasts its own spacious balcony or terrace. Guests enjoy access to complimentary wireless internet, a stocked mini-bar and 24-hour room service.

  • Junior Suite Spa or Pool View: Available with one king or two double-sized beds. Overlooks the swimming pool or spa area. 800 square foot.

Bar Services at Finest Playa Mujeres

Finest Playa Mujeres is home to 16 different bars and lounges staffed by top mixologists. All premium wine, beer, spirits and non-alcoholic drinks are included in the Finest Playa Mujeres package. Guests of Excellence and Finest Suites have access to four exclusive bars.

  • Golden Bar: Open all day, this popular bar offers coffees, cocktails, cervezas and more.
  • The Sweet Corner: A European-style café serving coffees and sweet treats.
  • Aqua Bar I: Guests can enjoy a frozen margarita without ever leaving the pool at this lively swim-up bar, featuring an impressive array of cocktails.
  • Aqua Bar II: The resort’s second swim-up bar, the underwater stools are a favourite amongst kids.
  • Las Dunas Beach House: Situated directly on the beach, this al-fresco bar specialises in tropical libations.
  • Piano Bar: Live music accompanies classic cocktails at this sophisticated adults-only bar.
  • Cigar Lounge: A spacious open-air smoking lounge, perfect for a night-cap.
  • El Teatro Spirits & More: A spot to mingle and grab a G&T before or after one of the evening’s performances.
  • El Teatro Tequilas & Mezcales: Adjacent to the theatre, this popular bar serves up some of Mexico’s finest tequila and mezcal cocktails.
  • Tropical Juice Bar: Freshly squeezed fruit juices and smoothies can be slurped in the shade of this health-conscious bar.
  • Sky Bar: Perhaps the resort’s trendiest drinking hole, fabulous views and complemented by contemporary décor and an excellent soundtrack.
  • Central Plaza: Located in the heart of the hotel, this is a central spot for coffee, tea, beers, mixed drinks and craft cocktails.

Dining at Finest Playa Mujeres

Encompassing twelve distinct restaurants, guests are taken on a culinary adventure around the globe. Meals can also be enjoyed in the privacy of suites, with complimentary 24-hour room service. Guests of Excellence and Finest Suites have access to an exclusive bistro.

  • The Market Kitchen: An international buffet inspired by the food halls of Europe.
  • Brass Steakhouse: A fine dining experience with an a la carte menu of prime cut steaks, salads, homemade pastas and an international wine list.
  • Surf & Turf: An elegant eatery serving chicken, shrimp, steaks and more.
  • Duke’s Lobster & Seafood: An outdoor terrace restaurant where American and Mexican culinary traditions combine.
  • Insieme Trattoria: A Tuscan-inspired trattoria featuring simple Italian favourites.
  • Tinto & Tapas: A Spanish-style restaurant offering tapas, sangrias and Iberian wines.
  • Lizo: A modern establishment with international dishes, Mexican delicacies and Tex-Mex treats.
  • Le Petit Plasir:s A fine-dining restaurant serving French fare with seasonal ingredients. Adults-only.
  • Las Dunas Beach House: A casual beach bar where wood-oven pizzas and grilled dishes can be enjoyed by the sea.
  • Legends: A snack shop where guests can fill up on burgers, sandwiches and salads in a relaxed setting.
  • Shoji: A diverse pan-Asian restaurant with three Teppanyaki stations and sushi bar.

Entertainment at Finest Playa Mujeres

Entertainment is a huge part of the Finest Playa Mujeres experience with live music from sun-up until the wee hours of the morning. Salsa, mariachi, folk and more can be found on the beach, by the pool and in restaurants. The resort also encompasses a theatre, where top performers delight audiences with diverse acts including magic shows, stand-up comedy and folklore retellings.

Activities at Finest Playa Mujeres

The all-inclusive, all-ages resort offers a range of activities to suit its diverse clientele. Along with 12 swimming pools and a 3,500-square foot fitness centre, guests can take part in volleyball, football, darts, billiards, water sports and more. Yoga, aerobics and dance lessons take place regularly and those inspired by the Finest Playa Mujeres’ dining experience can join a cooking lesson.

There’s no danger of little ones getting bored thanks to the resort’s array of baby, toddler, child and teen-friendly activities. Facilities include a zip-line, climbing wall, air hockey, complimentary bicycles, board games, kids’ library, playground, mini disco and cinema.

Excursions at Finest Playa Mujeres

Home to Mayan wonders, world-class golf courses and the delights of the Caribbean Sea, there’s plenty to see and do on the Yutucan Peninsula. Finest Playa Mujeres serves as the perfect launch pad for exploring this marvellous corner of Mexico and staff at the hotel can help you arrange a number of off-site excursions through their in-house agency. Trips include:

  • Chichen Itza Exclusive Tour
  • Dolphin Royal Swim
  • Pirate Dinner Show Captain Hook

Finest Playa Mujeres Spa

The One Spa is Finest Playa Mujere’s on-site wellness retreat and a total relaxation oasis. Facials, scrubs, massages and wrap treatments use both modern technology and ancient traditions for astounding results. There’s even a mini spa for kids, offering such services as lullaby massages and strawberry sparkling pedicures.

Weddings at Finest Playa Mujeres

Finest Playa Mujeres employs a dedicated team of wedding planning experts to help couples plan their big day in paradise. The bridal packages cover every detail and can be tailored with an endless list of special touches. Tie the knot with your toes in the sand, on a roof top terrace or amongst lush gardens. The resort’s romantic suites are ideal for honeymoons.
