Barbados Key Facts

Time Difference: -4 GMT

Capital: Bridgetown

Language: English (Official) and Bajan

 

Currency: Barbadian Dollar (BBD)

Flight Time: 8.5 Hours

Electricity: 110V, 50Hz (USA Plug)

When to visit Barbados
