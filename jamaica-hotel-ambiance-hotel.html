Club Ambiance

As you might expect from a resort called Club Ambiance, this adults-only retreat is all about atmosphere. Days are laidback and lazy while nights are lively and packed full of entertainment. The authentic Caribbean vibe, high-quality service and gorgeous location of Club Ambiance helps it enjoy one of the highest rates of repeat guests of any resort in the country. Your all-inclusive package allows access to food, drinks and entertainment.

Tucked amongst lush tropical gardens, Club Ambiance sits on Jamaica’s north coast in the parish of St Ann. The town of Ocho Rios is just a 30-minute drive away.

Rooms, Services, and Facilities. Decidedly Accommodating

Club Ambiance

Rooms at Club Ambiance

Club Ambiance is home to 85 guest rooms spread across five buildings. Each features a patio or balcony, the perfect spot for relaxing and enjoying the sunshine. Modern amenities, such as a satellite television, coffeemaker and fridge are provided.

  • Standard Rooms: Overlook the verdant gardens.
  • Superior Rooms: Overlook the pool and Caribbean Sea.
  • Deluxe Junior Suites: These enhanced rooms offer the best views in the resort with balconies that directly face the sea.

Bar Services at Club Ambiance

Guests can enjoy two bars and a disco club, with drinks and snacks included in the all-inclusive package. Sip a beer on a pool terrace, sample cocktails amongst the greenery of Coconut Bar or dance the night away at Fantasia Disco Club.

Dining Services At Club Ambiance

Club Ambiance’s restaurant features an ever-changing menu of local and international dishes. Guests can look forward to various theme nights, including a Jamaican barbeque with live music on the terrace.

Jamaican Hospitality

Jamaica’s renowned beauty, cuisine and warmth of spirit collide in this spectacular beachfront resort. Providing the opportunity for romance, relaxation and revelry, Club Ambiance is the perfect destination to unwind.

Entertainment at Club Ambiance

Club Ambiance holds regular live entertainment nights on its seafront terrace. Guests are invited to dance under the stars as professional musicians play soca, salsa, reggae and more.

Activities at Club Ambiance

While the focus is firmly on relaxation at this tranquil beach resort, there are plenty ways to get active on holiday. Complimentary pedalos, kayaks and snorkelling equipment are a great way to explore surrounding waters while volleyball courts, darts boards and an outdoor pool table get guests in the competitive spirit. Those looking to burn off that extra slice of rum cake can take an aerobics class, held both in the pool and in the gardens. Alternatively, relax on the beach - the resort is home to three, one of which is clothing-optional.

Excursions at Club Ambiance

Club Ambiance provides a free daily shuttle to the port town of Ocho Rios and the spectacular Dunn’s River Falls, one of the most scenic spots on the island and a major tourist attraction.

Club Ambiance Spa

The spa hut offers a number of high-quality treatments by trained professionals. Massages, facials, body scrubs, body wraps, aromatherapy and more can be enjoyed in a beautiful seafront location.

Weddings at Club Ambiance

The stunning natural surroundings and stress-free wedding packages offered by Club Ambiance make it popular spot to tie the knot. Loved-up couples can say “I do” by the edge of the Caribbean Sea in an intimate or lavish ceremony. All the essentials, such a wedding officiant, musical accompaniment and bouquet are provided. A wedding coordinator will help you plan your special day and can arrange most extras.
