Palms Resort Barbados

Barbados’ west coast is well-known for its breathtaking beaches, super-luxury resorts and, unfortunately, its high prices. The Palms Resort makes Barbados’ chic west coast accessible, offering high-quality accommodation at reasonable rates. The tranquil property encompasses two hotels, The Travellers Palm Hotel and The Halcyon Palm Hotel.

The Palm Resort sits amongst the splendour of Barbados’ west coast. The historical neighbourhood of Holetown is less than a mile away, home to an array of shops, restaurants and bars. The drive to Bridgetown takes just twenty minutes.

Rooms at Palms Resort Barbados

The Travellers Palm Hotel encompasses four One-Bedroom Apartments and 12 One-Bedroom Superior Apartments. Each apartment boasts its own balcony with pool or garden views, a separate living room and fully-equipped kitchen. Modern amenities such as cable television, a hairdryer and coffee-making facilities are provided. The newly refurbished apartments can accommodate up to four people.

The Halcyon Palm Hotel encompasses 25 One-Bedroom Apartments. Each apartment features a private balcony with pool or garden views, a separate living room and fully-equipped kitchen. Modern amenities such as cable television, a hairdryer and coffee-making facilities are provided. The apartments can accommodate up to three adults or two adults with two children.

Bar Services at Palms Resort Barbados

Guests can enjoy Caribbean and Continental cuisine in a relaxed setting at Safas Restaurant. Located in the lush gardens that surround the swimming pool, the casual eatery serves breakfast, lunch, dinner along with a wide range of refreshing beverages. The Bajan lunch special is particularly popular.

Safas Restaurant is located within The Travellers Palm but is accessible to all guests of the resort.

Entertainment at Palms Resort Barbados

A party is held each week at The Travellers Palm. Guests are invited to sample delicious cocktails and mingle.

AN ARRAY OF ACTIVITIES TO ENJOY

Both hotels boast their own swimming pools surrounded by loungers, where guests can relax, splash and sunbathe. The resort’s excellent location means that shopping centres, golf courses, tennis courts and water sport areas are all within easy reach.

Day passes are available to the Beach House Facility, located a few minutes’ walk away. The Facility includes a bar, restaurant, pool and beach access.

Excursions at Palms Resort Barbados

Exploring Barbados is easy at The Palms Resort thanks to its close proximity to a number of tourist attractions and local tour operators. A wide variety of nearby businesses offer both cultural and adventure excursions, such as scuba diving trips, catamaran charters and island tours.
