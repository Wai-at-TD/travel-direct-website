Excellence Playa Mujeres

Boasting five-star facilities and a location that defines “paradise”, Excellence Playa Mujeres delivers a luxury all-inclusive experience. The adults-only resort sits on a secluded two-mile stretch of white sand, yet the bustle and excitement of downtown Cancun is just a short drive away. Amongst the swaying palm trees, guests can discover seven swimming pools, ten international restaurants, eleven bars and a spa.
Watch the resort video and see for yourself. The low key, lively atmosphere at Excellence Playa Mujeres adds to its popularity as a destination for couples’ holidays. The beachfront location is the focal point of water based activities during the day and by night couples can relax and warm up by a late night beach bonfire under the stars.

Excellence Playa Mujeres sits on Mexico’s Caribbean coast in a self-contained resort community, home to an inland marina and the Greg Norman signature golf course. Cancun International Airport is just half-an-hour away.

Rooms at Excellence Playa Mujeres

Featuring fine furnishings, contemporary design features and top-of-the-line electronics, there a few better places to relax at the end of the day than an Excellence Playa suite. All accommodation includes a private seating area, flat-screen TV, DVD player, Jacuzzi and balcony or terrace. The full marble bathrooms include double showers, double vanities and designer amenities. A fully stocked minibar is provided but room service is available 24/7. Complimentary Wi-Fi can be accessed in every suite.

Bar Services at Excellence Playa Mujeres

This adults-only resort is home to 11 distinct bars, each with its own vibe and exciting drinks menu. Sip a poolside cocktail, chat over a cappuccino or kick back with cold beer. The all-inclusive package includes unlimited natural fruit juices, soft drinks and alcoholic beverages.

  • Cafékafé: An outdoor bistro with an extensive menu that will delight all coffee lovers.
  • Blue: A swim-up bar, guests can pause for a cool drink without leaving the water.
  • Sol: This popular pool bar is an ideal spot for sunbathing and slurping.
  • Cielo: Exclusively for Excellence Club members, bed-style seating makes this a luxurious place to relax.
  • Aroma: This coffee house serves up tasty hot drinks along with a wide variety of pastries, muffins, cakes and ice cream.
  • Stars: Guests can enjoy a drink and show at this theatre bar.
  • Sports Bar: Delicious snacks, cool beer and a big screen make this a popular spot to watch all the important games.
  • Xlounge: Situated directly on the beach, this chilled-out bar is the perfect place to take in the view.
  • Revive: Colourful smoothies are prepared with fresh seasonal fruit.
  • La Habana: A cosy setting for premium cigars and spirits.

Dining at Excellence Playa Mujeres

Excellence Playa Mujeres features ten different restaurants and a beach snack grill. Dishes are expertly prepared with fine quality ingredients to provide a gourmet experience. There’s plenty of healthy options and vegetarian dishes to choose from too. No reservations required.

  • Agave: A lively restaurant serving up authentic Mexican cuisine and Tex-Mex favourites. Guests can enjoy their meal in the romantic interior or on the outdoor hacienda-style courtyard. Tableside mariachi entertainment completes the experience. Open 6:00 pm – 10:30 pm.
  • Barcelona: Specialising in food from Spain and the Mediterranean, Barcelona’s chef prepares paella, veal, beef, seafood and an array of tasty tapas dishes. Open 6:00 pm – 10:30 pm.
  • Basmati: An elegant Indian restaurant specialising in food from the north-western region. Open 6:00 pm-10:30 pm.
  • Chez Isabelle: Offering sophisticated French cuisine, this fine-dining establishment has both indoor and outdoor seating with live music every night. Open 6:00 pm-10:30 pm.
  • The Grill: At lunch time, this casual eatery serves up salads, sandwiches, burgers and wraps. In the evening, guests can enjoy an alfresco dinner of steak, chicken or shrimp, cooked to perfection on an open flame. Lunch 12:00 pm - 6:00 pm, dinner 6:30 pm - 11:00 pm.
  • Flavour Market: A trendy restaurant with a relaxed vibe and mouth-watering tapas dishes. The expert sommelier will help you pair your food with wine. Open 6:00 pm - 12:00 am.
  • The Lobster House: Set in an open-air thatch-roof palapa, this relaxed restaurant offers shellfish dishes prepared precisely to guests’ tastes. Breakfast 8:00 am -12:00 pm, lunch 12:30 pm - 3:00 pm and dinner 6:00 pm - 11:00 pm.
  • Las Olas: Located on the beach, this popular grill offers fast food, salad, wood-oven pizzas and more. Open 12:00 pm– 3:00 pm.
  • Spice: Guests can transport their taste buds to the far east with dishes from Japan, China, Vietnam and Thailand. The restaurant encompasses a sushi bar and Teppanyaki rooms. Open 6:00 pm - 10:30 pm.
  • Toscana: This Italian restaurant features traditional dishes with contemporary twists. Breakfast and lunch are served buffet style while dinner is a la carte. Breakfast 7:00 am - 11:00 am, lunch 12:00 pm - 4:00 pm and dinner 6:00 pm - 10:30 pm

Entertainment at Excellence Playa Mujeres

Professional shows take place every night at Excellence Playa’s Star Bar, ranging from tribute acts to Mexican folklore enactments. No tickets or reservations are required, so guests can simply show up, order a cocktail and enjoy the performance. Live music takes place regularly across the resort restaurants and the Sports Bar screens all major sporting events. An entertainment calendar is detailed in the Excellence Times, delivered to every suite.

Activities at Excellence Playa Mujeres

Excellence Playa’s jam-packed schedule of activities is enough to drag even the most dedicated sun worshipper off their lounger. As well as boasting a state-of-the-art gym and sports facilities, the resort hosts a number of fitness classes including yoga, pilates, water aerobics and dance. Guests can even pick up a new skill on holiday with introductory scuba, Spanish and cooking lessons all on offer.

Excursions at Excellence Playa Mujeres

Complimentary bicycle tours and a number of other extra-cost excursions provide are a great way to get out and see more of this enchanting country. Excellence Playa offers shared or private deep-sea fishing charters, sunset cruises, golf trips, shipwreck dives, tastings and more.

Excellence Playa Mujeres Spa

Excellence Playa’s world-class spa and beauty parlour offers an array of indulgent treatments to relax and revive. Massages, body scrubs, facials and more are carried out by highly-trained professionals with deluxe products, many of which contain natural native ingredients.

Weddings at Excellence Playa Mujeres

Set on an unspoiled 20-mile peninsula, Excellence Playa is the perfect setting for a romantic Caribbean wedding. Couples can tie the knot in a stunning stone gazebo or on the beach itself before enjoying their reception at one of the hotel restaurants or celebrating on the sand. Excellence Playa offers a number of comprehensive wedding packages and professionals are on hand to help personalise the big day.
