Nestled amongst 240 acres of tropical forest, the Fairmont Mayakoba is a luxury resort where the environment reigns supreme. Vehicles are abandoned at the entrance of the property to maintain the resort's delicate ecology and all transport within is accomplished on lanchas (covered boats), electric golf carts, bicycles or on foot. The shoreline is just a short stroll away for most, though some exclusive hotel suites sit adjacent to the white-sand beach. The lush greenery and sparkling waterways are accompanied by five swimming pools, three bars, four eateries and a state-of-the-art spa.

The Fairmont Mayakoba sits in the centre of Playa del Carmen, an exclusive gated community within the “Venice of the Caribbean”, Riviera Maya. Bustling Cancun is a 50-minute drive away and the region's international airport can be reached in 35-minutes.

Fairmont Mayakoba: Luxury Resort

Five-star luxury meets the rich offering of mother nature at Fairmont Mayakoba, an environmentally conscious forest resort. The expansive grounds stretch to the Caribbean Sea and are punctuated by picturesque lagoons and mangroves along with deluxe facilities. The resort has won a number of awards for its eco-friendly policies and practices.

Fairmont Mayakoba sits within the Mexican state of Quintana Roo on the Caribbean coast. Several important Mayan archaeological sites exist in the area.

Rooms

Rooms are well appointed with modern facilities, luxurious amenities and stylish design features. All accommodation features large windows to let in the ample sunshine as well as a private outdoor area. The spacious bathrooms are equipped with a soaking tub and separate glass-wall shower.

This package includes the Fairmont Room - King. If you would like an upgrade to any of the different rooms or suites offered by the resort, please get in touch with your Travel Direct holiday specialist.

Activities

Boasting five swimming pools, a large spa, beach and gym, there’s plenty to keep guests busy at the Fairbank Mayakoba. A number of popular tourist attractions can be reached from the resort and a complimentary shuttle service runs to El Camaleón Golf Course and The Jim McLean Golf School. Fairbank Mayakoba also provides activities and areas specially designed for families, including a Kids Club and Teens Room.
