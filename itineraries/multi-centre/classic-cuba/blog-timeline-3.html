Varadero: Cuba's most popular beach.

With its crystal clear waters and powdery soft sands, Varadero is the ideal haunt for couples and families alike, and the perfect ending to your Classic Cuba Multi-Centre Holiday. A beach as vibrant underwater as it is above land, Varadero is the perfect place for relaxation and water based activities, and thus the ideal way to finish up a multi-centre holiday. Spend your basking in the beautiful rays of the sun, in an all-inclusive resort beach-front resort, scuba diving and snorkelling in the beautiful, clear sea, discovering thousands of varieties of fish and beautifully colourful coral.
